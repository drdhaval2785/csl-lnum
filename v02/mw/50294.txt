<s>kim</s> <hom>b</hom> ¦ is much used as a particle of interrogation like the <ab>Lat.</ab> <etym>num</etym>, <etym>an</etym>, sometimes translatable by ‘whether?’ but oftener serving only like a note of interrogation to mark a question (<ab>e.g.</ab> <s>kiM vyADA vane 'smin saMcaranti</s>, ‘do hunters roam about in this wood?’ In an interrogation the verb, if uncompounded with a preposition, generally retains its accent after <s>kim</s>, <ls>Pāṇ. 8-1, 44</ls>).
