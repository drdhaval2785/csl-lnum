BRĀHMAMUHŪRTA¦. The period of forty-eight minutes
<div n="lb"/>before the sunrise is called Brāhmamuhūrta. The
<div n="lb"/>deity of this period is Brahmā. In Agni Purāṇa, Chap-
<div n="lb"/>ter 155, it is instructed that one should wake up at
<div n="lb"/>this period and engage in prayer and meditation.
