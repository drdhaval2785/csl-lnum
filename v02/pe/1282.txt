BRAHMAVAIVARTAPURĀṆA¦. One of the eighteen
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇas. Sāvarṇi Manu taught this Purāṇa to Nārada.
<div n="lb"/>The theme of the Purāṇa is the story of Rathandhara.
<div n="lb"/>This Purāṇa contains eighteen thousand verses. It is
<div n="lb"/>stated in Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 272, that this Purāṇa
<div n="lb"/>is good to be given as a gift on the full moon day of the
<div n="lb"/>month of Māgha.
