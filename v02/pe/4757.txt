NARĀNTAKA I¦. A captain of the army of Rāvaṇa. It
<div n="lb"/>is stated in Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 10, that the captains of
<div n="lb"/>Rāvaṇa, viz., Kumbha, Nikumbha, Makarākṣa,
<div n="lb"/>Mahodara, Mahāpārśva, Matta, Unmatta, Praghāsa,
<div n="lb"/>Bhāsakarṇa, Virūpākṣa, Devāntaka, Narāntaka, Triśiras,
<div n="lb"/>Atikāya and other Rākṣasas fought on the side of
<div n="lb"/>Rāvaṇa against Śrī Rāma and that all of them were
<div n="lb"/>killed.
<div n="NI"/>Of these Devāntaka and Narāntaka were mighty and
<div n="lb"/>valiant warriors. Aṅgada killed Narāntaka after a
<div n="lb"/>fierce battle. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Yuddha Kāṇḍa,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 69).
