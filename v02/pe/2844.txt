<div n="lb"/>those who snatched away Amṛta from the hands of
<div n="lb"/>Dhanvantari. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 3).
<div n="NI"/>In the Purāṇas several Asuras (demons) bear-

[Page342-b+ 65]
<div n="lb"/>38, that Śrī Kṛṣṇa killed an Asura named Jambha.

<div n="lb"/>Chapter 98, Stanza 49 that the teacher Śukra refused
