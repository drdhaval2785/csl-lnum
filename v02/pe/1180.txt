<div n="lb"/>Ketumatī, by the Rākṣasa, Sumālī. They had ten sons—
<div n="lb"/>Prahasta, Akaṃpana, Vikaṭa, Kālakāmukha, Dhūmrā-
<div n="lb"/>kṣa, Daṇḍa, Supārśva, Saṃhrāda, Prākvāṭa, and
<div n="lb"/>Bhrāsakarṇa and four daughters—Vekā, Puṣpotkaṭā,
<div n="lb"/>Kaikasī and Kuṃbhīnasī. (Uttara Rāmāyaṇa).
