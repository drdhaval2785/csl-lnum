<div n="lb"/>information about him is gathered from Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Suśarman, son of Vṛddhakṣema attended Draupadī's
<div n="lb"/>wedding. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, Verse 9).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Once he incited Duryodhana to attack Matsya,
<div n="lb"/>the Virāṭa King. Accordingly Duryodhana attacked the
<div n="lb"/>Virāṭa city and Suśarman aided him in the battle.
<div n="lb"/>(Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 30).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) In the battle that followed the lifting of the cows
<div n="lb"/>of the Virāṭa King by the Kauravas, Suśarman took the
<div n="lb"/>Virāṭa King as captive. (Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 33,
<div n="lb"/>Verse 7).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) In the battle that followed the above incident
<div n="lb"/>Bhīma caught Suśarman as prisoner. (Virāṭa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 33, Verse 25).
<div n="NI"/>(v) At the instance of Yudhiṣṭhira Bhīma set Suśarman
<div n="lb"/>free. (Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 33, Verse 58)
<div n="NI"/>(vi) Suśarman fought against the Pāṇḍavas and on the
<div n="lb"/>first day of the great war he fought a duel with Ceki-
<div n="lb"/>tāna. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 45, Verse 60).
<div n="NI"/>(vii) Arjuna defeated Suśarman. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 82, Verse 1).
<div n="NI"/>(viii) He fought with Arjuna, Bhīma and Dhṛṣṭa-
<div n="lb"/>dyumna. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 114; Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 14).
<div n="NI"/>(ix) He vowed that he would kill Arjuna. (Droṇa
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 17, Verse 11).
<div n="NI"/>(x) When Droṇācārya was killed, he ran away from
<div n="lb"/>the battle-field. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter 193, Verse 18).
<div n="NI"/>(xi) Arjuna killed Suśarman. (Śalya Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>27, Verse 45).
<div n="NI"/>(xii) Names like Prasthalādhipa, Rukmaratha, Trai-
