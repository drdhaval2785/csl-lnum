ŚIVAPŪJĀ¦ (ŚIVA WORSHIP). There are certain injun-
<div n="lb"/>ctions with regard to the worship of Śiva. Nandikeśa and
<div n="lb"/>Mahākāla are to be worshipped first. Then should
<div n="lb"/>Gaṅgā, Yamunā, the gaṇas, Vāstupuruṣa, Śakti, and
<div n="lb"/>Dharma be worshipped. Next, the navaśaktis (nine
<div n="lb"/>powers) i.e. Vāmā, Jyeṣṭhā, Raudrī, Kālī, Kalavikariṇī
<div n="lb"/>(Kalavikalinī) Balavikariṇī (Balavikalinī) Balaprama-
<div n="lb"/>thinī, Sarvabhūtadamanī and Manonmanī should be
[Page731-b+ 65]
<div n="lb"/>worshipped. Then chanting the mantra ‘Hām, hūm,
<div n="lb"/>hām Śivamūrtaye’ Śiva is to be worshipped. Śiva is to
<div n="lb"/>be worshipped with aṅgas (with limbs, touching certain
<div n="lb"/>portions of the body) and with Vaktra (mouth, chant-
<div n="lb"/>ing). Aṅgas are to be worshipped with the chanting,
<div n="lb"/>Haum Śivāya haum’ Vaktra with the chanting ‘Hām
<div n="lb"/>Īśānāya’. Gaurī should be worshipped with the bīja
<div n="lb"/>‘Hrīm’, Gaṇapati with ‘Gam’ and Dikpālas like Indra,
<div n="lb"/>his arrows and Caṇḍeśvara should be worshipped by
<div n="lb"/>touching the heart etc. of the worshipper. (Agni Purāna,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 21).
