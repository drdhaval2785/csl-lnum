DAŚĀRHA¦. A king of the Yadu family. He was so famous
<div n="lb"/>that his descendants were called the Daśārhas. As Śrī
<div n="lb"/>Kṛṣṇa was born as a descendant in the line of Daśārha,
<div n="lb"/>Śrī Kṛṣṇa is sometimes called Dāśārha, in certain places.
<div n="lb"/>For genealogy see under Yaduvaṃśa.
