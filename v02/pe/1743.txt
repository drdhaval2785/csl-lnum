DARADA II¦. An ancient country in North East India.
<div n="lb"/>The people of this country were called the Daradas. It
<div n="lb"/>is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Sabhā Parva, Chapter 27,
<div n="lb"/>that Arjuna conquered this country during his conquest
<div n="lb"/>of countries. The Daradas paid tribute to Yudhiṣṭhira.
<div n="lb"/>During their forest-life the Pāṇḍavas had passed through
<div n="lb"/>the country of the Daradas. At the beginning of the
<div n="lb"/>Bhārata-battle, the Pāṇḍavas had sent invitation to the
<div n="lb"/>Daradas also. But they fought on the side of the Kauravas.
<div n="lb"/>Mention is made in Mahābhārata, Droṇa Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>70, Stanza 11 that Śrī Kṛṣṇa had conquered the Darada
<div n="lb"/>country. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 121, that in the battle of Bhārata the Daradas
<div n="lb"/>attacked Sātyaki and that Sātyaki killed them.
