VAIVASVATA¦ MANU. The seventh Manu. There is a
<div n="lb"/>description of Manu Vaivasvata under Manvantara.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu in the following
<div n="lb"/>order:—Brahmā-Marīci-Kaśyapa-Vivasvān-Vaivasvata
<div n="lb"/>Manu.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%The incarnation of Matsya and Vaivasvata Manu.%} See
<div n="lb"/>under Avatāra, Section “Matsya.”
<div n="NI"/>3) {%Wife and children.%} The wife of Vaivasvata Manu
<div n="lb"/>was Śraddhā. Many sons were born to the couple.
<div n="lb"/>Prominent among them were, Yama, Yamī, Aśvinī-
<div n="lb"/>kumāras, Revanta, Sudyumna, Ikṣvāku, Nṛga, Śaryāti,
<div n="lb"/>Diṣṭa, Dhṛṣṭa, Karūṣa, Nariṣyanta, Nābhāga, Pṛṣadhra
<div n="lb"/>and Kavi.
