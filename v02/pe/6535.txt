SAVITĀ¦. One of the twelve sons of Kaśyapaprajāpati
<div n="lb"/>by his wife called Aditi. These sons are called Ādityas.
<div n="lb"/>So, Savitā also is one of the Dvādaśādityas. Viṣṇu,
<div n="lb"/>Śakra, Aryamā, Dhātā, Tvaṣṭā, Pūṣā, Vivasvān, Savitā,
<div n="lb"/>Mitra, Varuṇa, Aṃśu and Bhaga are the Dvādaśā-
<div n="lb"/>dityas.
<div n="NI"/>Pṛśnī, wife of Savitā, gave birth to three daughters
<div n="lb"/>called Sāvitrī, Vyāhṛti and Trayī and three sons called
<div n="lb"/>Agnihotra, Paśusoma and Cāturmāsya the great
<div n="lb"/>sacrifices. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Part 1, Chapter 15).
