ŚIKHAṆḌĪ¦ (ŚIKHAṆḌINĪ I). Rebirth of Ambā,
<div n="lb"/>daughter of the king of Kāśī. Ambā ended her life with
<div n="lb"/>the vow that she would take revenge on Bhīṣma and was
<div n="lb"/>born in the next life as the daughter of king Drupada
<div n="lb"/>under the name Śikhaṇḍinī, and Śikhaṇḍinī helped
<div n="lb"/>Arjuna in the war at Kurukṣetra to kill Bhīsma. (For
<div n="lb"/>the history of Ambā ending in two lives see under Ambā.
<div n="lb"/>Certain pieces of information not included under that
<div n="lb"/>head are given below).
<div n="NI"/>(1) Śikhaṇḍinī was present at the wedding of Abhi-
<div n="lb"/>manyu in the city of Upaplavya. (Virāṭa Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>72, Verse 17).
<div n="NI"/>(2) Śikhaṇḍī learned archery at the feet of Droṇācārya.
<div n="lb"/>(Udyoga Parva, Chapter 192, Verse 60).
<div n="NI"/>(3) On the first day of the great war a duel was fought
<div n="lb"/>between Śikhaṇḍī and Aśvatthāmā. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 45, Verse 46).
<div n="NI"/>(4) Śīkhaṇḍī retreated from battle-field in fear of
<div n="lb"/>Droṇa. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 69, Verse 31).

<div n="NI"/>(5) A second duel was fought by him and Aśvatthāmā.
<div n="lb"/>(Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 82, Verse 26).
<div n="NI"/>(6) Śikhaṇḍī broke the arrow of Śalya with his divine
<div n="lb"/>arrow (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 85, Verse 29).
<div n="NI"/>(7) He tried to kill Bhīṣma. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>108, Verse 45).
<div n="NI"/>(8) Śikhaṇḍī attacked Bhīṣma at the instance of Arjuna.
<div n="lb"/>(Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 110, Verse 1).
<div n="NI"/>(9) He followed that attack with many other attacks.
<div n="lb"/>(Bhīṣma Parva, Chapters 114, 118 and 119).
<div n="NI"/>(10) Sañjaya praised the prowess of Śikhaṇḍī to Dhṛta-
<div n="lb"/>rāṣṭra. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter 10, Verse 45).
<div n="NI"/>(11) Bhūriśravā and Śikhanḍī fought with each other.
<div n="lb"/>(Droṇa Parva, Chapter 14, Verse 43).
<div n="NI"/>(12) Śikhaṇḍī and Karṇa fought with each other.
<div n="lb"/>(Droṇa Parva, Chapter 26, Verse 7).
<div n="NI"/>(13) Śikhaṇḍī and Bālhīka fought with each other.
<div n="lb"/>(Droṇa Parva, Chapter 96, Verse 7).
<div n="NI"/>(14) In the fight that followed, Śikhaṇḍī was defeated
<div n="lb"/>by Kṛtavarman. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter 114, Verse 82).
<div n="NI"/>(15) Kṛpācārya defeated Śikhaṇḍī. (Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 169, Verse 22).
<div n="NI"/>(16) In the battle that followed with Kṛtavarman,
<div n="lb"/>Śikhaṇḍī fainted. (Karṇa Parva, Chapter 26, Verse 26).
<div n="NI"/>(17) Defeated by Kṛpācārya, Śikhaṇḍī had to run away
<div n="lb"/>for life. (Karṇa Parva, Chapter 54).
<div n="NI"/>(18) Karṇa defeated Śikhaṇḍī. (Karṇa Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>61, Verse 7).
<div n="NI"/>(19) Annihilating the army of Prabhadrakas Śikhaṇḍī
<div n="lb"/>fought fiercely with Kṛtavarman and Kṛpa. (Śalya
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 15, Verse 7).
<div n="NI"/>(20) Śikhaṇḍī resisted the advance of Aśvatthāmā.
<div n="lb"/>(Śalya Parva, Chapter 16, Verse 6).
<div n="NI"/>(21) In the fierce battle that was fought after the fall
<div n="lb"/>of Bhīṣma Aśvatthāmā killed Śikhaṇḍī. (Sauptika Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 8, Verse 65).
<div n="NI"/>(22) Synonyms of Śikhaṇḍī used in Mahābhārata.
<div n="lb"/>Bhīṣmahantā, Śikhaṇḍinī, Draupadeya, Drupadātmaja,
