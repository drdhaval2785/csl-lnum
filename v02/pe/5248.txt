<div n="lb"/>named Antardhāna and Vādī. Antardhāna got a son
<div n="lb"/>named Havirdhāna of his wife Śikhaṇḍinī. Dhiṣaṇā of

<div n="lb"/>got of Havirdhāna six sons named Prācīnabarhis, Śukra,
<div n="lb"/>Gaya, Kṛṣṇa Vraja and Ajina. (Chapter 14, Aṃśa 1,

<div n="lb"/>Pracetas. They were Prajāpatis. Dakṣa was born of the ten
<div n="lb"/>Prajāpatis to Vārkṣī alias Māriṣā. Therefore he got the
