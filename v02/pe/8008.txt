VARADĀNA¦. A holy place near Dvārakā. It was here
<div n="lb"/>that Durvāsas gave Bhagavān Śrī Kṛṣṇa boons (Vara).
<div n="lb"/>From that day this became a holy place. It is mentioned
<div n="lb"/>in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 82, that those
<div n="lb"/>who take bath in this holy place would obtain the
<div n="lb"/>fruits of giving a thousand cows as alms.
