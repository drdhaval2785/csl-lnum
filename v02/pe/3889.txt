KURUJIT¦. A king born in King Janaka's dynasty. Aja
<div n="lb"/>was his father and Ariṣṭanemi his son. The genealogy
<div n="lb"/>from Janaka to Kurujit is as follows: Janaka—Vīrada—
<div n="lb"/>Nandivardhana—Suketu—Devarāta—Bṛhaddhṛta—
<div n="lb"/>Mahāvīra—Dhṛtaketu—Haryaśva—Maru—Pratisvaka-
<div n="lb"/>Kraturatha—Devamīḍha—Vidhṛta—Mahādhṛta—
<div n="lb"/>Kṛtirāta—Mahāromā—Svarṇaromā—Prastharomā—
<div n="lb"/>Sīradhvaja (Janaka, the father of Sītā)—Kurudhvaja
<div n="lb"/>—Dharmadhvaja—Kṛtadhvaja—Keśiketu—Bhānumān
<div n="lb"/>—Śakradyumna—Śuci—Vanadhvaja—Ūrjaketu—Aja
<div n="lb"/>—Kurujit. (Bhāgavata, 9th Skandha).
