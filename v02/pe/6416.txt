SATRĀJIT¦. A King of the Yādavas. It is mentioned in
<div n="lb"/>Bhāgavata Skandha 9, that he was the son of Nimna
<div n="lb"/>and the brother of Prasena. (To know the previous
<div n="lb"/>birth of Satrājit see under Satyabhāmā). Śrī Kṛṣṇa
<div n="lb"/>married Satyabhāmā the daughter of Satrājit. In
<div n="lb"/>connection with the jewel Syamantaka, Kṛtavarmā
[Page703-a+ 64]
<div n="lb"/>caused Satrājit to be murdered. (More details about
<div n="lb"/>Satrājit occur under the entries Prasena, Kṛtavarmā,
<div n="lb"/>(Para 2) and Satyabhāmā).
