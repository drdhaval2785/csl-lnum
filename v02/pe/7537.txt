TEJOVATĪ¦. The capital city of Agni. This is situated on
<div n="lb"/>the south-east corner of Mahāmeru. In the centre is
<div n="lb"/>Manovatī, capital city of Brahmā. To the east of it is
<div n="lb"/>Amarāvatī, capital city of Indra. In the south-east
<div n="lb"/>corner is Tejovatī. In the south is Saṃyamanī, city of
<div n="lb"/>Yama. In the south-west is Kṛṣṇāñjanī of Nirṛti. In
<div n="lb"/>the west is Śraddhāvatī of Varuṇa. In the north west
<div n="lb"/>is Gandhavatī of Vāyu. In the north is Mahodayā of
<div n="lb"/>Kubera. In the north east corner is Yaśovatī of Īśāna.
<div n="lb"/>(8th Skandha, Devī Bhāgavata).
