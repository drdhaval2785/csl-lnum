PRABHADRAKA¦. A special division of the army of
<div n="lb"/>Pāñcālarāja. This division comprises of Kṣatriyas only.
<div n="lb"/>Dhṛṣṭadyumna and Śikhaṇḍī were its leaders. During the
<div n="lb"/>Kurukṣetra battle Śalya slew many of the Prabhadrakas.
<div n="lb"/>The rest of the Prabhadrakas were killed by Āśvatthāmā
<div n="lb"/>while they were sleeping. (Chapter 8, Sauptika Parva;
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 57. Udyoga Parva; Chapter 19, Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 11, Śalya Parva).
