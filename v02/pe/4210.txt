MAHODAYA¦. One of the sons of Vasiṣṭha. Once
<div n="lb"/>Triśaṅku, King of Ayodhyā, conducted a yāga with
<div n="lb"/>Viśvāmitra as Ṛtvik and for that yāga Viśvāmitra
<div n="lb"/>invited Vasiṣṭha and his son Mahodaya also. But
<div n="lb"/>Mahodaya refused to respond to the invitation and
<div n="lb"/>sent a message picturing both Viśvāmitra and
<div n="lb"/>Triśaṅku as Caṇḍālas. Enraged at this insulting
<div n="lb"/>message Viśvāmitra cursed Mahodaya to become
<div n="lb"/>Śūdra. (Verses 20-21, Bāla Kāṇḍa, Chapter 59, Vālmīki
<div n="lb"/>Rāmāyaṇa).
