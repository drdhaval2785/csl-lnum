VADHRIMATĪ¦. A princess, praised in Ṛgveda. This
<div n="lb"/>beautiful princess got a hermaphrodite as her husband.
<div n="lb"/>She shed a good deal of tears and prayed to the Aśvins
<div n="lb"/>(Charioteers of Indra, celebrated as Gods of light and
<div n="lb"/>helpers) in consequence of which she got a son named
<div n="lb"/>Hiraṇyahasta. (Ṛgveda, Maṇḍala 1, Śūkta 116).
