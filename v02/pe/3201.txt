KAMBAR¦. A celebrated Tamil poet. He was born in a
<div n="lb"/>poor family. At the orders of the King many poets wrote
<div n="lb"/>the story of Rāmāyaṇa in Tamil. But Kambar's work
<div n="lb"/>was accepted as the best. This is the renowned Kamba
<div n="lb"/>Rāmāyaṇa. Kambar has written many other poems of
[Page381-b+ 65]
<div n="lb"/>which the important ones are Sarasvatī Antādi and
<div n="lb"/>Kāñcī Purāṇa. It is said that unintelligent children of
<div n="lb"/>Tamil nādu are even now given sand from the burial
<div n="lb"/>ground of Kambar to induce intelligence into them.
<div n="lb"/>The (—r) suffix in Kambar is plural denoting respect.
