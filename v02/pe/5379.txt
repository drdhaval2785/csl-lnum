PRATIVINDHYA I¦. A son born to Pāñcālī of Dharma-
<div n="lb"/>putra. The details available about him from Mahā-
<div n="lb"/>bhārata are the following:—
<div n="NI"/>(i) Prativindhya was born from a part of a Viśvadeva.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 127, Chapter 37, Ādi Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) On the first day of the Kurukṣetra battle Prativin-
<div n="lb"/>dhya fought against Śakuni. (Śloka 63, Chapter 45,
<div n="lb"/>Bhīṣma Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Prativindhya was defeated in fight with Alambuṣa
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 39, Chapter 100, Bhīṣma Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) He fought against Aśvatthāmā. (Śloka 29, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>25, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Prativindhya accepted defeat after fighting with
<div n="lb"/>Duśśāsana. (Śloka 34, Chapter 168, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(vi) He slew king Citra in a battle. (Śloka 20,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 14, Karṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(vii) Prativindhya died fighting A vatthāmā at night.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 48, Chapter 8, Sauptika Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(viii) The synonyms found used in the Mahābhārata for
<div n="lb"/>Prativindhya are the following:—Yaudhiṣṭhira and
<div n="lb"/>Yaudhiṣṭhiri.
