MADANAMĀLĀ¦. A celebrated prostitute of the city of
<div n="lb"/>Pratiṣṭhāna. Narasiṃha, King of Pratiṣṭhāna, did not
<div n="lb"/>surrender himself to Vikramāditya, emperor of
<div n="lb"/>Pāṭalīputra. The resourceful Vikramāditya went to
<div n="lb"/>Pratiṣṭhāna and caught hold of the prostitute Madana-
<div n="lb"/>mālā first and then through her subdued the King
<div n="lb"/>Narasiṃha. After that Vikramāditya took Madana-
<div n="lb"/>mālā to his place and made her stay there. (Taraṅga
<div n="lb"/>4, Ratnaprabhālambaka, Kathāsaritsāgara).
