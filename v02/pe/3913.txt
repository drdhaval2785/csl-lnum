KUŚĀMBA II¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} A son of Kuśa. The city he built was
<div n="lb"/>called Kauśāmbī. (See under Kuśa).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%} (i) Kuśāmba had a daughter
<div n="lb"/>called Cārvaṅgī, who was married by Bhadraśreṇya,
<div n="lb"/>the solar King. (Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa, Chapter 2).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Two sons, Śakra and Gādhi were born to Kuśāmba
<div n="lb"/>and to Gādhi was born a daughter, Satyavatī. Viśvā-
<div n="lb"/>mitra was Gādhi's son. (Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>57).
<div n="NI"/>This statement is not quite correct. Certain other
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇas maintain that Gādhi, the father of Viśvāmitra
<div n="lb"/>was the son of Kuśanābha, who was the son of Kuśika
<div n="lb"/>and brother of Kuśāmba. (See under Kuśanābha).
