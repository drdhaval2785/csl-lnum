ĀYUṢMĀN¦. Kaśyapa, son of Marīci, got two sons,
<div n="lb"/>Hiraṇyākṣa and Hiraṇyakaśipu and a daughter Siṃhikā,
<div n="lb"/>of his wife Diti. Siṃhikā became the wife of Vipracitti.
<div n="lb"/>Of her were born the celebrated asuras, Saiṃhikeyas,
<div n="lb"/>of which Rāhu was one. Hiraṇyakaśipu had four brave
<div n="lb"/>sons, Anuvrāta, Vrāta, Prahlāda and Saṃhrāda. Of
<div n="lb"/>these Vrāta got a son Vrata, Saṃhrāda got Āyuṣmān,
<div n="lb"/>Śibi, and Bāṣkala and Prahlāda got a son, Virocana.
<div n="lb"/>Mahābali was the son of Virocana. (Chapter 19, Agni
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇa).
