BALĀKṢA¦. An ancient king of Bhārata. While the
<div n="lb"/>Pāṇḍavas were living incognito in the kingdom of Virāṭa,
<div n="lb"/>Duryodhana and his brothers stole the cows of Virāṭa,
<div n="lb"/>in consequence of which there was a battle. The devas
<div n="lb"/>(gods) came in planes to see the fight between Arjuna
<div n="lb"/>and the teacher Kṛpa. It is mentioned in the Mahā-
[Page102-b+ 64]
<div n="lb"/>bhārata, Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 56, that the King Balākṣa
<div n="lb"/>was there with the gods when they came to see the fight.
