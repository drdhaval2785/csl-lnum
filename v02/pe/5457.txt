PULINDA¦(S) I. The people of the country of Pulinda.
<div n="lb"/>Information available regarding them from the Mahā-
<div n="lb"/>bhārata is given below:
<div n="NI"/>(1) Pulindas were originally Kṣatriyas. But they be-
<div n="lb"/>came Śūdras by a curse of the brahmins. (Chapter 33,
<div n="lb"/>Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(2) Pulindas who belong to the mleccha tribe became
<div n="lb"/>rulers in Kaliyuga. (Chapter 186, Vana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(3) Pulindas were born from the foam of the celestial
<div n="lb"/>cow Nandinī of sage Vasiṣṭha when she got enraged.
<div n="lb"/>(Chapter 165, Āśramavāsika Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(4) Bhīma fought against the Pulindas and destroyed
<div n="lb"/>all their big cities. (Chapter 26, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(5) In the great Mahābhārata battle Pulindas fought
<div n="lb"/>on the side of Duryodhana. (Chapter 158, Udyoga
<div n="lb"/>Parva).
