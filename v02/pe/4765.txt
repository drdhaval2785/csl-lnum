NĀRĀYAṆASTHĀNA¦. (ŚĀLAGRĀMATĪRTHA). A
<div n="lb"/>sacred place where Mahāviṣṇu is always present.
<div n="lb"/>Brahmā, Devas, Sannyāsins, Ādityas, Vasus. and Rudras
<div n="lb"/>are also ever present here in the service of Viṣṇu.
<div n="lb"/>Viṣṇu is called. Śālagrāma as he is being thus worshipped
<div n="lb"/>at Śālagrāma. Those who visit the place will enjoy the
<div n="lb"/>fruits of Aśvamedha yajña and ascend to Vaikuṇṭha.
<div n="lb"/>(Vana Parva, Chapter 84, Verse 115).
