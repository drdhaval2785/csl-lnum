<div n="NI"/>The hell called Avīci is described thus: “This is the

<div n="lb"/>top of a mount which is a hundred yojanas (league) high.

<div n="lb"/>ing and falling and swaying and surging always. When
<div n="lb"/>sinners fall there their bodies will be crumbled to pieces.
