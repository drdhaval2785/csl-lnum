<div n="lb"/>in the country called Pārvatīya in ancient Bhārata were
<div n="lb"/>called Pārvatīyas. Pārvatīyas were also present for the

<div n="lb"/>fight against Jayadratha during their exile in the fo-
<div n="lb"/>rests these pārvatīyas fought on the side of Jayadratha.
<div n="lb"/>Arjuna killed them all. In the Kurukṣetra battle the
<div n="lb"/>Pārvatīyas fought on the side of the Kauravas. Pārvat-
<div n="lb"/>īya was a very important country of ancient Bhārata.

<div n="lb"/>against the Pāṇḍavas under the banners of Śakuni and
<div n="lb"/>Ulūka. The Pāṇḍavas destroyed them all. (Chapter 52,
