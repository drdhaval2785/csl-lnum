MĀTAṄGĪ¦. The great grandmother of the elephants.
<div n="lb"/>Mātaṅgī was the daughter of Krodhavaśā, daughter of
<div n="lb"/>Dakṣa and wife of Kaśyapaprajāpati. Mātaṅgī had nine
<div n="lb"/>sisters. Elephants were born of Mātaṅgī. (Sarga 14,
<div n="lb"/>Araṇya Kāṇḍa; Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
