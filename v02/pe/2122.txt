DṚḌHĀŚVA¦. A famous king of the Ikṣvāku dynasty.
<div n="lb"/>He was the son of Kuvalayāśva who had 21,000 sons.
<div n="lb"/>All those sons but three were killed in the fight with an
<div n="lb"/>asura called Dhundhu. The three sons who survived
