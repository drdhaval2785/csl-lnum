KALPAVṚKṢA¦. A tree in Devaloka. It has the
<div n="lb"/>power of giving any object that one wishes to get.
<div n="lb"/>There are five Kalpavṛkṣas in Devaloka. Their names
<div n="lb"/>are: Mandāra, Pārijāta, Santāna, Kalpavṛkṣa and
<div n="lb"/>Haricandana.
<div n="NI"/>Agni Purāṇa, third Chapter mentions that among the
<div n="lb"/>wonderful things obtained by the churning of the
<div n="lb"/>ocean of milk, there was Kalpavṛkṣa also. So Kalpa-
<div n="lb"/>vṛkṣa was born from the ocean of milk.
