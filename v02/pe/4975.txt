PAÑCANADA I¦. A land of the north-western side of
<div n="lb"/>Bhārata. This is at present called the Punjab. When
<div n="lb"/>Nakula conquered the western states he conquered
<div n="lb"/>Pañcanada also. (Śloka 11, Chapter 32, Mahābhārata).
<div n="lb"/>Five rivers of names Vipāśā (Vyāsa), Śatadrū (Sutlej),
<div n="lb"/>Irāvatī (Rāvī), Cndrabhāgā (Chenab) and Vitastā
<div n="lb"/>(Jhelum) run through this place and that is why the
<div n="lb"/>place is called Pañcanada.
