ŚUKA VII¦. A Maharṣi who was the contemporary of
<div n="lb"/>Aṇuha of Dakṣiṇa Pāñcāla and of king Brahmadatta.
<div n="lb"/>This sage lived before the time of the other Śuka who
<div n="lb"/>was the son of Vyāsa.
<div n="NI"/>This sage Śuka had six sons, named Bhūriśravas,
<div n="lb"/>Śambhu, Prabhu, Kṛṣṇa, Saura (Sauraprabha) and
<div n="lb"/>Devaśruta by his two wives Pīvarī and Ekaśṛṅgā.
<div n="lb"/>(Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa, 3-8-93: Vāyu Purāṇa, 70-84;
<div n="lb"/>Devī Bhāgavata, 1-14; Nārada. 1-58).
