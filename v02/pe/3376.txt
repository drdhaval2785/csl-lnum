KARŪṢA¦ (M). A place in ancient India. Historians
<div n="lb"/>are of opinion that it is the Bundelkhaṇḍa of modern
<div n="lb"/>India. The sin of Brahmahatyā (killing of brahmins)
<div n="lb"/>of Indra was washed away by brahmins at this place.
<div n="lb"/>The place where Karīṣa (cowdung) from Indra fell
<div n="lb"/>was called Karīṣa and it gradually became Karūṣa.
<div n="lb"/>(See under Aṅgamalaja).
