PRAHUTA¦. A great yajña.
<div n="NI"/>Manusmṛti states that respecting each of the following
<div n="lb"/>five is considered as a yajña in itself:—A devatā, a guest,
<div n="lb"/>servants, parents and the self. Of these the first is
<div n="lb"/>called Āhuta, second, huta, third, prahuta, fourth
<div n="lb"/>brāhmyahuta and fifth, prāśita (Ślokas 72. 73, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>3, Manusmṛti).
