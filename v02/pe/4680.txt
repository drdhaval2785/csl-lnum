NAIDHRUVA¦. A gotra-originator of the Kaśyapa dynasty.
<div n="lb"/>He was the grandson of Maharṣi Kaśyapa and son of
<div n="lb"/>Avatsara maharṣi. He was one of the six Brahmavādins
<div n="lb"/>of the family of Kaśyapa the other five being Kaśyapa,
<div n="lb"/>Avatsara, Raibhya, Asita and Devala. (Vāyu Purāṇa,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 52; Matsyapurāṇa, Chapter 145).
