BAHURŪPA¦. One of the eleven Rudras. Eleven
<div n="lb"/>Rudras were born to Kaśyapa by his wife Surabhi.
<div n="lb"/>Surabhi, who had been purified by Maheśvara whom
<div n="lb"/>she had pleased by her penance, got as sons, Aja,
<div n="lb"/>Ekapād, Ahirbudhnya, Tvaṣṭṛ and Rudra. The renown-
<div n="lb"/>ed Viśvarūpa was the son of Tvaṣṭṛ. The eleven
<div n="lb"/>Rudras are Hara, Bahurūpa, Tryambaka, Aparājita,
<div n="lb"/>Vṛṣākapi, Śambhu, Kapardin, Raivata, Mṛgavyādha,
<div n="lb"/>Sarpa and Kapālin The number of the Rudras is one
<div n="lb"/>hundred lakhs. They pervade everything moving and
<div n="lb"/>not moving. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 18).
