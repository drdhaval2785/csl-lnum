<div n="lb"/>possesses different forms and aspects. Pārvatī, spouse of
<div n="lb"/>Śiva is a form of Durgā. According to the prayer of
<div n="lb"/>devotees this Devī assumes diverse forms. She is

<div n="lb"/>Ambikā, Bhadrakālī, Bhadrā, Kṣemākṣemakarī, Naika-

<div n="lb"/>Kāmākṣī and Mūkāmbā. In Kerala the Devī is called

<div n="lb"/>nadu Kaṇṇakī. Worship of the Devī is supposed to be

<div n="lb"/>important place. In modern India Śrī Rāmakṛṣṇa
