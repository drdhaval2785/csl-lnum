KṢATRADEVA¦. The son of Śikhaṇḍī. He was a
<div n="lb"/>famous archer. Bhagadatta wounded the right hand of
<div n="lb"/>Kṣatradeva in the Bhārata-battle. He fought with
<div n="lb"/>Lakṣmaṇa and Droṇa. Lakṣmaṇa killed him.
<div n="lb"/>(M. B. Udyoga Parva, Chapters 57 and 171; Bhīṣma
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 95; Droṇa Parva, Chapters 14, 21 and
<div n="lb"/>23; Karṇa Parva, Chapter 6).
