YĀYĀVARA¦(S). A particular section of Brahmins.
<div n="lb"/>Their special feature is that they wander about here
<div n="lb"/>and there, following the course of life of the sages.
<div n="lb"/>Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 13, mentions that
<div n="lb"/>the reputed sage Jaratkāru was a Yāyāvara.
