<div n="lb"/>and Balabhadra were enjoying pleasures with naked
<div n="lb"/>Gopastrīs at Vṛndāvana Śaṅkhacūḍa, attracted by the

<div n="lb"/>in the fight that ensued was killed by Kṛṣṇa, who gave
