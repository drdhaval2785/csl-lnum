MĀRKAṆḌEYA¦ (M). A famous holy place. This
<div n="lb"/>place is situated about sixteen miles to the north of
<div n="lb"/>Kāśī at a place where the rivers Gaṅgā and Gomatī
<div n="lb"/>meet. He who visits this place would get the benefit
<div n="lb"/>of doing an Agniṣṭomayajña. Not only that, he would
<div n="lb"/>lift the prestige of his family. (Śloka 80, Chapter 34,
<div n="lb"/>Vana Parva).
