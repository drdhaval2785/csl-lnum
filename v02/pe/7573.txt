TRETĀYUGA¦. Second of the four yugas. Kṛtayuga,
<div n="lb"/>Tretāyuga, Dvāparayuga, and Kaliyuga are the four
<div n="lb"/>yugas. There are three thousand deva-varṣas (divine
<div n="lb"/>years) in Tretāyuga.
<div n="NI"/>Śrī Rāma was born at the end of Tretāyuga. Tretāyuga
<div n="lb"/>came to an end in B.C. 867100. Śrī Rāma ruled the
<div n="lb"/>country for eleven thousand years.
