REVATĪ III¦. One of the twentyseven stars. The following
<div n="lb"/>statements occur in the Mahābhārata about the import-
<div n="lb"/>ance of this star.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Śrī Kṛṣṇa started on his journey at the auspicious
<div n="lb"/>moment of Maitra on the star Revatī in the month of
<div n="lb"/>Kārttika. (M.B. Udyoga Parva. Chapter 83, Stanza 6).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) If a cow is given as alms on the day of this star
<div n="lb"/>that cow will go to heaven and make preparations for
<div n="lb"/>the comforts and convenience of the giver. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 64, Stanza 33).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) He who gives offerings to the manes on Revatī
<div n="lb"/>day would become wealthy. (M.B. Anuśāsana Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 89, Stanza 14).
