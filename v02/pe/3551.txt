KIRĀTĀRJUNĪYA¦. During the life in exile in the
<div n="lb"/>forest of the Pāṇḍavas, Arjuna performed penance to
<div n="lb"/>propitiate Śiva in the Himālayas. Disguised as a
<div n="lb"/>forest-hunter Śiva appeared on the scene and tested
<div n="lb"/>Arjuna's valour. Arjuna won in the test and was
<div n="lb"/>presented the Pāśupatāstra by Śiva. The story is told
<div n="lb"/>in Chapter 167 of the Vana Parva. (See under
<div n="lb"/>Arjuna).
