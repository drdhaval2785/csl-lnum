PRĀCINVĀN¦. A king who was the son of Janamejaya
<div n="lb"/>and grandson of Pūru. His mother's name was Anantā.
<div n="lb"/>This king conquered in one day all the countries in the
<div n="lb"/>east (prācī) and got his name Prācinvān. He got of his
<div n="lb"/>wife Aśmakī a son named Saṃyāti. (Śloka 12, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>95, Ādi Parva). Prācinvān got a son named Manasyu.
<div n="lb"/>(Chapter 278, Agni Purāṇa).
