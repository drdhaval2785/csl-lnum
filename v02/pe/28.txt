ADHĪRA¦. A King; also a great devotee of Lord Śiva.
<div n="lb"/>Once he decreed death penalty on an innocent woman.
<div n="lb"/>He also destroyed with his own hands a temple of Śiva.
<div n="lb"/>As a consequence of those two sinful deeds he became
<div n="lb"/>a devil after death. Ultimately by the grace of Śiva
<div n="lb"/>he cast off the devil's form and became an attendant of
<div n="lb"/>Śiva. (Padma Purāṇa, Pātāla Khaṇḍa, Chapter 111).
