CEKITĀNA¦. (SĀTVATA, VĀRṢṆEYA).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} An archer of the Vṛṣṇi dynasty.
[Page181-b+ 65]
<div n="lb"/>He lived for the Pāṇḍavas and attained Vīra Svarga
<div n="lb"/>(Heaven for the brave) in the great battle.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Important events.%} (1) Cekitāna was present at the
<div n="lb"/>svayaṃvara of Draupadī. (Chapter 171, Śloka 18,
<div n="lb"/>Udyoga Parva).
<div n="NI"/>2) When the Pāṇḍavas entered the hall constructed by
<div n="lb"/>Maya for the first time Cekitāna was with them.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 27, Chapter 4, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>3) At the time of the Rājasūya he approached Dharma-
<div n="lb"/>putra and presented him with an arrow-holder. (Śloka 9,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 53, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>4) On the first day of the great battle this great archer
<div n="lb"/>wrestled with Suśarmā. (Śloka 60, Chapter 45, Bhīṣma
<div n="lb"/>Parva).
<div n="NI"/>5) At the Kurukṣetra in the combat with Kṛpācārya
<div n="lb"/>both of them fainted. (Śloka 31, Chapter 84, Bhīṣma
<div n="lb"/>Parva).
<div n="NI"/>6) He wrestled with Citrasena. (Śloka 8, Chapter 110,
<div n="lb"/>Bhīṣma Parva).
<div n="NI"/>7) Cekitāna fought with Anuvinda in the Kurukṣetra
<div n="lb"/>battle. (Śloka 48, Chapter 14, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>8) Droṇācārya defeated Cekitāna in the great battle.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 68, Chapter 125, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>9) Duryodhana killed Cekitāna in the great battle.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 31, Chapter 12, Śalya Parva).
<div n="NI"/>10) When Vyāsa by his yogic powers invited the spirits
<div n="lb"/>of all the dead warriors on the banks of the Gaṅgā the
<div n="lb"/>spirit of Cekitāna was also there. (Śloka 12, Chapter 32,
<div n="lb"/>Āśramavāsika Parva).
