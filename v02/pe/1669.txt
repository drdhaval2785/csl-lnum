DAKṢASĀVARṆI¦. The name of the ninth Manu.
<div n="lb"/>During the regime of this Manu, there will be three
<div n="lb"/>classes of devas (gods) called the Parṇas, the Marīci-
<div n="lb"/>garbhas and the Sudharmans. Each of these gaṇas or
<div n="lb"/>classes will consist of twelve devas or gods. Indra, their
<div n="lb"/>King will be known as Adbhuta. He will be mighty and
<div n="lb"/>powerful. In that Manvantara (age of the Manu)
<div n="lb"/>Savana, Dyutimān, Bhavya, Vasu, Medhātithi,
<div n="lb"/>Jyotiṣmān and Satya will be the Saptarṣis (the seven
<div n="lb"/>hermits) and Dhṛtaketu, Dīptiketu, Pañcahasta, Nirā-
<div n="lb"/>maya, Pṛthuśrava and others will be the sons of Manu,
<div n="lb"/>Dakṣasāvarṇi. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1, Chapter 2).
