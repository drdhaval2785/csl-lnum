DEVARĀTA III¦. A house-holder whose daughter Kalā
<div n="lb"/>was married by Śoṇa. Kalā was killed by Mārīca.
<div n="lb"/>Devarāta and Śoṇa along with Viśvāmitra went to
<div n="lb"/>Śivaloka in search of Kalā. As Kalā had, at the time
<div n="lb"/>of her death, uttered the word ‘Hara’ (Śiva) she had
<div n="lb"/>gone to Mount Kailāsa and was spending her days in the
<div n="lb"/>service of Pārvatī who, after making Kalā and Śoṇa
<div n="lb"/>participate in Somavāravrata sent them back to earth.
<div n="lb"/>(Padma Purāṇa, Pātāla Khaṇḍa, Chapter 112).
