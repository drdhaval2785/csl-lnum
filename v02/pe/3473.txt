<div n="lb"/>the son of Vasudeva.
<Poem>“Pauravī Rohiṇī Bhadrā
<div n="lb"/>Madirā Rocanā Ilā /
<div n="lb"/>Devakīpramukhā āsan

<div n="NI"/>Pauravī, Rohiṇī, Bhadrā, Madirā, Rocanā, Ilā and
<div n="lb"/>Devakī were the wives of Vasudeva.
<div n="NI"/>From this statement made in Bhāgavata, Skandha 9,

<div n="lb"/>wives. Kausalyā, who was one of them, was the mother
<div n="lb"/>of Keśī. (Bhāgavata, Skandha 9, Chapter 24, Stanza
<div n="lb"/>48).
