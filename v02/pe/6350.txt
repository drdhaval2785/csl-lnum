ŚARYĀTI I¦. A son of Vaivasvata Manu.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} Ikṣvāku, Nābhāga, Dhṛṣṭa, Śaryāti,
<div n="lb"/>Nariṣyanta, Prāṃśu, Nṛga, Diṣṭa, Karūṣa and Pṛṣa-
<div n="lb"/>dhra were sons of Vaivasvata Manu. Śaryāti had a son
<div n="lb"/>called Ānarta and a daughter called Sukanyā, who
<div n="lb"/>was married by the aged and blind Cyavana, and a
<div n="lb"/>son named Pramati was born to the couple. (See under
<div n="lb"/>Cyavana).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Śaryāti lives in Yama's court worshipping him.
<div n="lb"/>(Sabhā Parva, Chapter 8, Verse 14).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Cyavana performed for Śaryāti his yajña at which
<div n="lb"/>the Aśvinīkumāras, in disobedience of Indra, drank
<div n="lb"/>Somarasa. (Vana Parva, Chapter 124).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Two famous Kings, Haihaya and Tālajaṅgha
<div n="lb"/>were born in Śaryāti's dynasty. (Anuśāsana Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 30, Verse 6).
