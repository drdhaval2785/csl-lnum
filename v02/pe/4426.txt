MARUTVATĪ¦. The mother of two Marutvāns. This
<div n="lb"/>Marutvatī was the daughter of Dakṣa, and the wife
<div n="lb"/>of Dharma. The ten daughters of Dakṣa, namely, Arun-
<div n="lb"/>dhatī, Vasu, Yāmī, Lambā, Bhānu, Marutvatī, Saṅkalpā,
<div n="lb"/>Muhūrtā, Sādhyā, and Viśvā were the wives of Dharma-
<div n="lb"/>deva. Viśvadevas were born to Viśvā, Siddhas were born
<div n="lb"/>to Sādhyā, Marutvāns were born to Marutvatī, Vasus
<div n="lb"/>were born to Vasu, Bhānus were born to Bhānu, Ghoṣa
<div n="lb"/>was born to Lambā and Nāgavīthī was born to Yāmī
<div n="lb"/>(Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Part I, Chapter 15).
