PHENAPA¦. A gotra-maker of the Bhṛgu family.
<div n="lb"/>Phenapa is mentioned in the context of describing to
<div n="lb"/>Yudhiṣṭhira the greatness of cows. Phenapa's original
<div n="lb"/>name was Sumitra. He lived on the shores of the river
<div n="lb"/>Kulajā in the mountain of Triśikhara drinking the
<div n="lb"/>foam of cow's milk alone and thus getting for him the
<div n="lb"/>name Phenapa. (Phena = foam; Pā = drink). (Anuśāsana
<div n="lb"/>Parva, M.B.)
