JANĀRDANA I¦. A synonym of Mahāvīṣṇu (Śrī Kṛṣṇa).
<div n="lb"/>Because he made the Dasyus (Asuras—demons) tremble,
<div n="lb"/>Śrī Kṛṣṇa was called Janārdana.
<div n="NI"/>“He, who has lotus as his seat, who is eternal, imperi-
<div n="lb"/>shable and unchangeable, he who has all these attribu-
<div n="lb"/>tes is Puṇḍarīkākṣa (lotus-eyed) and he who makes the
<div n="lb"/>Asuras tremble is Janārdana.” (M.B. Udyoga Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 70, Stanza 6).
