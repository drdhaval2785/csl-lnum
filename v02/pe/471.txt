ARYAMĀ¦. One of the twelve Ādityas born to Kaśyapa
<div n="lb"/>and Aditi. (Ādityas = sons of Aditi). The twelve
<div n="lb"/>Ādityas are, Dhātā, Aryamā, Mitra, Śakra, Varuṇa,
<div n="lb"/>Aṃśa, Bhaga Vivasvān, Pūṣā, Savitā, Tvaṣṭā and
<div n="lb"/>Viṣṇu. (M.B., Ādi Parva, Chapter 65, Stanza 15).
<div n="lb"/>Aṃśa is sometimes called AṂŚU.
