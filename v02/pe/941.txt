<div n="lb"/>living there were called Barbaras. They were consider-
<div n="lb"/>ed as low caste people. There is also a statement that
<div n="lb"/>these people were born of the sides of the cow, Nan-
<div n="lb"/>dinī. (Śloka 37, Chapter 174, Ādi Parva, M.B.).
<div n="lb"/>During the victory march of the Pāṇḍavas Bhīmasena
<div n="lb"/>conquered the Barbaras. Nakula also conquered these
<div n="lb"/>people. (Chapter 32, Sabhā Parva, M.B.). In the
<div n="lb"/>Rājasūya yajña of Dharmaputra the Barbaras were
<div n="lb"/>present with gifts.
