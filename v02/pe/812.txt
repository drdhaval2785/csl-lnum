<div n="lb"/>born to Mahāmeru, Āyati and Niyati. Āyati was married
<div n="lb"/>by Dhātā and Niyati by Vidhātā. Dhātā and Vidhātā
<div n="lb"/>are the two sons born to Bhṛgu Maharṣi of his wife
<div n="lb"/>Khyāti. Dhātā got a son, Prāṇa, of Āyati and Vidhātā,

<div n="lb"/>Purāṇa).
