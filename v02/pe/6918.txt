<div n="lb"/>Bharata, the son of Ṛṣabha, had married Pañcajanī,
<div n="lb"/>the daughter of Viśvarūpa. Five sons named Sumati,
<div n="lb"/>Rāṣṭrabhṛt, Sudarśana, Āvaraṇa and Dhūmraketu were
