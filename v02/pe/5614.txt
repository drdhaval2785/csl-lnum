RĀJAGṚHA¦ (GIRIVRAJA). An ancient city in India,
<div n="lb"/>capital of Magadha. Dīrgha King of Girivraja was killed
<div n="lb"/>by King Pāṇḍu. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 112, Verse 27)
<div n="lb"/>Another King, Ambuvīca, had for a time ruled over the
<div n="lb"/>city. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 203, Verse 17). Afterwards
<div n="lb"/>Jarāsandha became its King. Girivraja is a holy place
<div n="lb"/>as well. He who bathes here will become as pleasant-
<div n="lb"/>looking as King Kakṣīvān. (Vana Parva, Chapter 84,
<div n="lb"/>Verse 104).
