VIŚVĀNARA¦. A King. For a time this King was
<div n="lb"/>miserable as he was childless. But by the blessing of
<div n="lb"/>Śiva, Viśvānara got a son named Gṛhapati by his wife
<div n="lb"/>Śucismitā. Gṛhapati was destined to have life only up
<div n="lb"/>to three years. But it is mentioned in Skanda Purāṇa that
[Page877-a+ 64]
<div n="lb"/>within this short period Gṛhapati learned the whole of
<div n="lb"/>Sāṅgaveda and obtained long life from Śiva.
