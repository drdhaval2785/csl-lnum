SATYADHṚTI II¦. A warrior who fought in chariot on
<div n="lb"/>the side of the Pāṇḍavas in the Battle of Bhārata. The
<div n="lb"/>following information is given about him in Mahā-
<div n="lb"/>bhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Bhīṣma considered Satyadhṛti as one of the
<div n="lb"/>prominent fighters in chariots. (M.B. Udyoga Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 17, Verse 18).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) This Satyadhṛti had been present at the Svayaṃ-
<div n="lb"/>vara marriage of Draupadī. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>185, Verse 10).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) This Satyadhṛti who was the son of Sucitra conti-
<div n="lb"/>nued helping Ghaṭotkaca the son of Hiḍimbī, during
<div n="lb"/>the battle of Bhārata. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>93, Verse 13).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Satyadhṛti was an expert in the wielding of all
<div n="lb"/>weapons, especially in archery. He was a scholar in the
[Page705-b+ 65]
<div n="lb"/>Vedas and Vedāṅgas. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>23).
<div n="NI"/>(v) In the Bhārata-battle he was killed by Droṇā-
<div n="lb"/>cārya. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 6, Verse 34).
