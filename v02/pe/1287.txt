BRĀHMAHUTAM¦. A sacrifice. There are five kinds of
<div n="lb"/>sacrifice, known as Āhuta, Huta, Prahuta, Brahma-
<div n="lb"/>huta and Prāśita. Āhuta is sacrifice to Brahmā, Huta
<div n="lb"/>is sacrifice to devas or Gods, Prahuta is sacrifice to
<div n="lb"/>Ghosts, Brahmahuta is sacrifice to man and Prāśita is
<div n="lb"/>the sacrifice to the manes. (Manusmṛti, Chapter 3).
