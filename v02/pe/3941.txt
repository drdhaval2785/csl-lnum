KUVALAYĀPĪḌA¦. Name of the elephant posted at
<div n="lb"/>the gates of Mathurāpurī to kill Śrī Kṛṣṇa and
<div n="lb"/>Balabhadrarāma, who went there to witness the
<div n="lb"/>dhanuryajña. But, they killed the elephant.
<Poem>Raṅgadvāraṃ samāsādya
<div n="lb"/>Tasmin nāgamavasthitam /
<div n="lb"/>Apaśyat Kuvalayāpīḍaṃ
<div n="lb"/>Kṛṣṇo smbaṣṭhapracoditam //</Poem>
<div n="lb"/>(Bhāgavata, Daśama Skandha, Chapter 48, Verse 2).
