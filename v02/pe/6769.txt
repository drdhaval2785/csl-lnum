ŚRUTAKĪRTI II¦. A son of Arjuna. Pāñcālī had five
<div n="lb"/>sons by the five Pāṇḍavas. Of these Prativindhya was
<div n="lb"/>the son of Yudhiṣṭhira; Śruta was the son of Bhīmasena;
<div n="lb"/>Śrutakīrti was the son of Arjuna; Śrutakarmā was the
<div n="lb"/>son of Sahadeva and Śatānīka was the son of Nakula.
<div n="lb"/>(Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 278). Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 67, Verse 127 mentions that this Śrutakīrti was
<div n="lb"/>born from a portion of Viśvadeva. In the Bhārata
<div n="lb"/>battle he had fought against Jayatsena and the son of
<div n="lb"/>Duśśāsana. In the end he died by the arrow of Aśvat-
<div n="lb"/>thāmā. (M.B. Sauptika Parva, Chapter 8, Verse 61).
