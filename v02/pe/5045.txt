PARIHYAṄGA¦. A maharṣi. One of the sons born to
<div n="lb"/>Rṣi Marīci of his wife Ūrṇā. There were six sons and
<div n="lb"/>the five others were: Smara, Udgītha, Kṣudrabhṛt,
[Page572-b+ 64]
<div n="lb"/>Agniṣvātta and Ghṛṇī. In his next birth he was born
<div n="lb"/>as one of the relatives of Śrī Kṛṣṇa and was killed by
<div n="lb"/>Kaṃsa. (10th Skandha, Bhāgavata).
