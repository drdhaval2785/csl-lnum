ŚRUTĀYUS II¦. (ŚRUTĀYUDHA.) A Kaliṅga king.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Birth.%} Śrutāyus was son of Varuṇa by Parṇāśā
<div n="lb"/>(For more details see under Parṇāśā).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) He was a member of Yudhiṣṭhira's assembly. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Sabhā Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 26).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Śrutāyus participated in Yudhiṣṭhira's Rājasūya
<div n="lb"/>yajña and presented precious stones to him. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Dākṣiṇātya Pāṭha, Sabhā Parva, Chapter 51).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) He had attended Draupadī's Svayaṃvara. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, Verse 13)
<div n="NI"/>(iv) In Bhārata Yuddha, he joined the Kaurava camp
<div n="lb"/>and assumed command of an Akṣauhiṇī (a division of the
<div n="lb"/>army). (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 76, Verse 16).
<div n="NI"/>(v) A fierce fight took place between Śrutāyus and
<div n="lb"/>Bhīmasena in the field of Kurukṣetra. (M.B. Bhīṣma
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 54, Verse 67).
<div n="NI"/>(vi) In the above battle, Satya and Satyadeva who were
<div n="lb"/>the supporters of Śrutāyus, were slain by Bhīmasena.
<div n="lb"/>(M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 54, Verse 76).

<div n="NI"/>(vii) Another battle was fought between Śrutāyus and
<div n="lb"/>Arjuna. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 92, Verse 36).
<div n="NI"/>(viii) Śrutāyus was killed by his own cudgel. (Mahā-
<div n="lb"/>bhārata, Droṇa Parva, Chapter 92, Verse 54). (See
<div n="lb"/>also under Śrutāyudha).
