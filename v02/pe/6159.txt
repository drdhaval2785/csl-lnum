ŚANI¦ (ŚANAIŚCARA-SATURN).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} Śani is the son of Sūrya by Chāyā, the
<div n="lb"/>maid-servant of Saṃjñā. Sūrya had three sons by
<div n="lb"/>Chāyā, Manu, Tapatī and Śani. (See under Chāyā).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Śani worships Brahmā in Brahmasabhā. (Sabhā
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 11, Verse 29).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Śani is very effulgent and intense in his form and
<div n="lb"/>character. When Śani threatens the star Rohiṇī, great
<div n="lb"/>disasters occur on earth. (Udyoga Parva, Chapter 143,
<div n="lb"/>Verse 8).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Śani will incarnate as Manu in the coming Yuga.
<div n="lb"/>(Śānti Parva, Chapter 349, Verse 55).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Śani is one of the planets to be daily worshipped.
<div n="lb"/>(Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 165, Verse 17).
<div n="NI"/>(v) In the battle between Śiva and Tripura, Śani
<div n="lb"/>mounted in a chariot clashed with Narakāsura. (Bhāga-
<div n="lb"/>vata, 6th Skandha).
