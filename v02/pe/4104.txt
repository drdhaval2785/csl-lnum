MAHĀGONA¦. A river (Nada) celebrated in the Purāṇas.
<div n="lb"/>It is also known as “Śoṇabhadra” and Mahāśoṇa. It is
<div n="lb"/>after crossing this river that Kṛṣṇa, Bhīma and Arjuna
<div n="lb"/>entered Magadha, the capital of Jarāsandha. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Sabhā Parva, Chapter 20, Verse 27).
