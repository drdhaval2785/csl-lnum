BHŪKAMPA¦. (Earthquake). The cause of earth-
<div n="lb"/>quake according to ancient belief is given below:—
<div n="lb"/>The earth is being supported and held in its position by
<div n="lb"/>an elephant called Virūpākṣa. When he feels the burden
<div n="lb"/>too heavy he shakes his head and earth tremors follow.
<div n="lb"/>(Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Bālakāṇḍa, 40th Sarga).
