UTTARAPĀÑCĀLA¦. An ancient country in Bhārata.
<div n="lb"/>Drupada became the King of this country on the death
<div n="lb"/>of King Pṛṣata. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 129, Stanza
<div n="lb"/>43). In course of time Uttarapāñcāla came under the
<div n="lb"/>control of the Ācārya Droṇa. (See under Droṇa). In the
<div n="lb"/>Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 137, Stanzas 70 to
<div n="lb"/>76 it is mentioned that this country was on the north
<div n="lb"/>bank of the Gaṅgā.
