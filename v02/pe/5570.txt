PUṢPOTKAṬĀ¦. Mother of Kubera.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} From Brahmā were born two demons
<div n="lb"/>Heti and Praheti. Heti married Bhayā, daughter of
<div n="lb"/>Kāla and to them was born a son named Vidyutkeśa.
<div n="lb"/>Vidyutkeśa married Sālakaṭaṅkā daughter of Sandhyā
<div n="lb"/>and to them was born a son named Sukeśa. They
<div n="lb"/>abandoned the child in a forest and went away. Śiva
<div n="lb"/>took care of the child Sukeśa who married Daivavatī
<div n="lb"/>daughter of the Gandharva called Maṇimaya and she
<div n="lb"/>delivered three sons named Mālyavān, Sumālī and
<div n="lb"/>Mālī and all the three started living in the crystal
<div n="lb"/>palace built at Laṅkā by Viśvakarmā.
<div n="NI"/>At that time a Gandharva woman called Narmadā
<div n="lb"/>delivered three daughters named Sundarī, Ketumatī
<div n="lb"/>and Vasudhā. When they came of age Sundarī was
<div n="lb"/>married to Mālyavān, Ketumatī to Sumālī and
<div n="lb"/>Vasudhā to Mālī. Mālyavān got of Sundarī seven
<div n="lb"/>demons named Vajrapuṣṭi, Virūpākṣa, Durmukha,
<div n="lb"/>Suptaghna, Yajñakeśa, Matta and Unmatta and a
<div n="lb"/>demoness of name Nalā. Sumālī got of Ketumatī ten
<div n="lb"/>sons named Prahasta, Akampana, Vikaṭa, Kālakā-
<div n="lb"/>mukha, Dhūmrākṣa, Daṇḍa, Supārśva, Saṃhrāda,
<div n="lb"/>Prakvāta and Bhāsakarṇa and four daughters named
<div n="lb"/>Vekā, Puṣpotkaṭā, Kaikasī and Kumbhīnasī. Mālī got
<div n="lb"/>of Vasudhā four sons named Anala, Anila, Aha and
<div n="lb"/>Sampāti.
<div n="NI"/>Sumālī wandered in the forests with his daughters. He
<div n="lb"/>gave all the four daughters in marriage to Viśravas,
<div n="lb"/>son of Pulastya. Viśravas got of Kaikasī three sons
<div n="lb"/>named Rāvaṇa, Kumbhakarṇa and Vibhīṣaṇa and a
<div n="lb"/>daughter named Śūrpaṇakhā. Viśravas got of Puṣpotkaṭā
<div n="lb"/>a son named Vaiśravaṇa or Kubera. (Chapter 11,
<div n="lb"/>Agni Purāṇa; Chapter 275, Vana Parva, Uttara
<div n="lb"/>Rāmāyaṇa).
