PIṆḌĀRAKA II¦. A sacred place situated near
<div n="lb"/>Dvārakā in Saurāṣṭra. He who bathes in a holy river
<div n="lb"/>there would get immense gold. The place is worshipped
<div n="lb"/>by the sages. He who stays there for one day bathing
<div n="lb"/>in that river would get the benefit of conducting an
<div n="lb"/>Agniṣṭomayajña. (Chapter 82, Śloka 62, Vana Parva;
<div n="lb"/>Śloka 21, Chapter 88, Vana Parva; Śloka 57, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>25, Anuśāsana Parva).
