CANDRĀPĪḌA¦. A son of Janamejaya. Parīkṣit was the
<div n="lb"/>son of Abhimanyu (Arjuna's son). Janamejaya was
<div n="lb"/>Parīkṣit's son. Janamejaya married princess Vapuṣṭamā
<div n="lb"/>of Kāśi. Two sons called Candrāpīḍa and Sūryāpīḍa
<div n="lb"/>were born to the couple. Candrāpīḍa had hundred sons,
<div n="lb"/>who distinguished themselves as great heroes in archery.
<div n="lb"/>Satyakarṇa was the eldest among the sons. Śvetakarṇa,
<div n="lb"/>son of Satyakarṇa, married Yādavī, the daughter of
<div n="lb"/>Sucāru. (Bhaviṣya Purāṇa).
