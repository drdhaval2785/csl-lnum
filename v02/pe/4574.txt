<div n="lb"/>Kaśyapaprajāpati. Kaśyapaprajāpati, son of Marīci,
<div n="lb"/>married the eight daughters of Dakṣa named Tāmrā etc.
<div n="lb"/>From Tāmrā were born birds. Krodhavaśā gave birth
