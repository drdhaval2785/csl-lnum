AṂŚUMĀN¦. Name of a King belonging to the Solar
<div n="lb"/>dynasty (Sūrya Vaṃśa).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Aṃśumān was descended from Viṣṇu
<div n="lb"/>through Brahmā-Marīci-Kaśyapa-Vivasvān-Vaivasvata-
<div n="lb"/>manu-Ikṣvāku-Vikukṣi-Śaśāda-Purañjaya-Kakutstha-
<div n="lb"/>Anenas-Pṛthulāśva-Prasenajit-Yuvanāśva-Māndhātā-
<div n="lb"/>Purukutsa-Trasadasyu-Anaraṇya-Aryaśvan-Vasuma-
<div n="lb"/>nas-Sudhanvā-Traiyyāruṇa-Satyavrata-Triśaṅku-Hariś-
<div n="lb"/>candra-Rohitāśva-Harita-Cuñcu-Sudeva-Bharuka-
<div n="lb"/>Bāhuka-Sagara-Asamañjas-Aṃśumān.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Sagara, a king of the Sūrya vaṃśa, (mention-
<div n="lb"/>ed above) had two wives, Sumati (Vaidarbhī) and
<div n="lb"/>Keśinī (Saibā) by name. Keśinī gave birth to a son
<div n="lb"/>named Asamañjas. Aṃśumān was the son of Asamañjas
<div n="lb"/>and the father of the famous Bhagīratha. (See under
<div n="lb"/>the title, BHAGĪRATHA).
<div n="NI"/>3) {%Some other details.%} Aṃśumān was one of the Kings
<div n="lb"/>who attended the Svayaṃvara of Pāñcālī. (Vide
<div n="lb"/>Śloka 11, Chapter 185 of Ādi Parva of the Mahā-
<div n="lb"/>bhārata).
