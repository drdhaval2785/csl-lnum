KUMUDVATĪ II¦. Wife of Kuśa, son of Śrī Rāma.
<div n="lb"/>Kuśa lost the ornaments on his hands in water once
<div n="lb"/>while he was sporting in the river. Angry at the loss
<div n="lb"/>Kuśa was about to shoot his arrows at the Sarayū when
<div n="lb"/>the nāga called Kumuda not only returned to him his
<div n="lb"/>lost ornaments but also gave Kumudvatī as wife to him.
<div n="lb"/>(Ānanda Rāmāyaṇa).
