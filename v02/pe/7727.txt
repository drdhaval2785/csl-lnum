<div n="lb"/>(Things to be offered to the guest). They are called
<div n="lb"/>Ṣoḍaśopacāras. They are given below:—(1) Āsana
<div n="lb"/>(seat) (2) Pādya (water to wash feet (3) Arghya (water

<div n="lb"/>other fragrant things for besmearing) (6) Dhūpa
<div n="lb"/>(smoke) (7) Dīpa (light) (8) Naivedya (food) (9)
<div n="lb"/>Tāmbūla (Betel) (10) Śītalajala (cool drinks) (11)
<div n="lb"/>Vasana (clothing) (12) Bhūṣaṇa (ornaments) (13)
<div n="lb"/>Mālya (garland) (14) Gandha (sweet-smelling things)
<div n="lb"/>(15) Ācamanīyaka (water to rinse mouth) (16) Sutalpa
<div n="lb"/>(Good bed).
<div n="NI"/>These are the sixteen offerings that we have to give to
