MERUDEVĪ¦. A daughter of Mahāmeru. The nine
<div n="lb"/>daughters of Mahāmeru are: Merudevī, Pratirūpā,
<div n="lb"/>Ugradaṃṣṭrī, Latā, Ramyā, Śyāmā, Nārī, Bhadrā and
<div n="lb"/>Devavīthī. They were married to Nābhi, Kimpuruṣa,
<div n="lb"/>Hari, Ilāvṛta, Ramyaka, Hiraṇmaya, Kuru, Bhadrāśva
<div n="lb"/>and Ketumāla respectively. All these nine Kings were
<div n="lb"/>born to a King Agnīdhra of the nymph Pūrvacitti.
<div n="lb"/>(Pañcama Skandha, Bhāgavata).
