VĀTA¦. One of the Saptarṣis (seven hermits) of the
<div n="lb"/>Manvantara (Age of the Manu) of Manu Svārociṣa.
<div n="lb"/>In this age of the Manu the Devendra was Vipaścit.
<div n="lb"/>The Saptarṣis of that Age of the Manu were Ūrja,
<div n="lb"/>Stamba, Prāṇa, Vāta, Vṛṣabha, Niraya and Parīvān.
<div n="lb"/>(Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 3, Chapter 1).
