DADHIVAKTRA¦. A monkey who helped Śrī Rāma.
<div n="lb"/>It is seen in Adhyātma Rāmāyaṇa, Sundara Kāṇḍa,
<div n="lb"/>Sarga 5, as follows:—
<div n="NI"/>“Dadhimukhaḥ Kruddhasugrīvasya mātulaḥ”. From
<div n="lb"/>this it is understood that Dadhimukha or Dadhiva-
<div n="lb"/>ktra was the uncle of Sugrīva. (For further infor-
<div n="lb"/>mation see Madhuvana).
