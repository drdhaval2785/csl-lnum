<div n="lb"/>going to hell by taking the vow called Viṣṇupañcaka.
<div n="lb"/>This Śūdra who lived in the tretāyuga had done a good
<div n="lb"/>deal of wicked deeds. Finally he accepted the advice of
<div n="lb"/>Brāhmaṇas and performed the vow of Viṣṇupañcaka
<div n="lb"/>and because of the vow he attained heaven. (Padma
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇa, Chapter 23).
