SŪRYAVAṂŚA¦. An important dynasty of ancient
<div n="lb"/>Bhārata. It is stated in Devībhāgavata, Skandha 7, as
<div n="lb"/>follows about the origin of this Solar dynasty.
<div n="NI"/>The Lord of creation Brahmā originated from the lotus
<div n="lb"/>in the navel of Viṣṇu. That Brahmā did penance for
<div n="lb"/>ten thousand years and pleased Parāśakti, the great
<div n="lb"/>goddess of power, and started creation. First of all he
<div n="lb"/>created seven mental sons. Of them Marīci became
<div n="lb"/>expert in creation. Prajāpati Kaśyapa the son of
<div n="lb"/>Marīci became a greater expert. The Sun is the son of
<div n="lb"/>Kaśyapa. Nine sons named Ikṣvāku, Nābhāga, Dhṛṣṭa,
<div n="lb"/>Śaryāti, Nariṣyanta, Prāṃśu, Nṛga, Diṣṭa, Karūṣa and
[Page773-a+ 63]
<div n="lb"/>Pṛṣadhra were born to the Sun. Of these Ikṣvāku be-
<div n="lb"/>came King. This line of Kings born from the Sun is
<div n="lb"/>called Sūryavaṃśa (Solar dynasty). (See the Genea-
<div n="lb"/>logy).
