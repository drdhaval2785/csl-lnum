VIṢṆUPURĀṆA¦. One of the eighteen Purāṇas. Viṣṇu
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇa is the description of the activities in Varāha
<div n="lb"/>Kalpa (the age of Varāha—Boar). There are twenty-
<div n="lb"/>three thousand granthas in this Purāṇa. It is stated in
<div n="lb"/>Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 272, that if this book Viṣṇu
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇa is given as a gift along with cow and water on
<div n="lb"/>the full moon day of the month of Āṣāḍha, the giver
<div n="lb"/>would attain the city of Viṣṇu. (For further details see
<div n="lb"/>under Purāṇa).
