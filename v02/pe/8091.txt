<div n="lb"/>by his wife Vaidarbhī. Kuśa had four sons, Kuśāmba,
<div n="lb"/>Kuśanābha, Asūrtarajas and Vasu. Of them Kuśāmba
<div n="lb"/>built the city of Kauśāmbī, Kuśanābha the city of
<div n="lb"/>Mahodayapura, Asūrtarajas the city of Dharmāraṇya and
<div n="lb"/>Vasu the city of Girivraja which is erected in the middle

<div n="lb"/>of five hills. The river Māgadhī flows around this city.
