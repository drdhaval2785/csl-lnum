AKARKKARA¦. A serpent, son of Kadru.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu in this order:
<div n="lb"/>Brahmā-Marīci-Kaśyapa-Akarkkara.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Kaśyapa married the eight daughters of
<div n="lb"/>Dakṣa named Aditi, Diti, Danu, Kālikā, Tāmrā,
<div n="lb"/>Krodhavaśā, Manu and Analā. Krodhavaśā got ten
<div n="lb"/>sons. One of them is Kadru. Ananta and Akarkkara
<div n="lb"/>were born of Kadru. (Śloka 16, Chapter 35, Ādi Parva,
<div n="lb"/>M.B.).
