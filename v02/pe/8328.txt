VIKARṆA I¦. One of the hundred sons of Dhṛtarāṣṭra.
<div n="lb"/>The following information is available in Mahābhārata
<div n="lb"/>about this valiant fighter.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Vikarṇa, the son of Dhrtarāṣṭra was one of the
<div n="lb"/>eleven mahārathas. (great warriors). (M.B. Ādi Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 63, Stanza 39).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Among the disciples of Droṇa, who attacked Dru-
<div n="lb"/>pada, Vikarṇa was one. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 137,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 19).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Vikarṇa was present at the Svayaṃvara (marriage)
<div n="lb"/>of Draupadī (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, Stanza 1).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Vikarṇa was one of those kings who stood silent
<div n="lb"/>and sad, being unable to answer the questions put by
<div n="lb"/>Draupadī when she was about to be stripped of her
<div n="lb"/>clothes. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 68, Stanza 1).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Once Karṇa reviled at Vikarṇa. (M.B. Sabhā Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 68, Stanza 80).
<div n="NI"/>(vi) At the time of the theft of the cows of king
<div n="lb"/>Virāṭa by Duryodhana and his brothers. Vikarṇa
<div n="lb"/>fought against Arjuna. (M.B. Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 54,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 9).
<div n="NI"/>(vii) A severe fight followed in which the defeated
<div n="lb"/>Vikarṇa fled from the battle-ground. (M.B. Virāṭa
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 54, Stanza 41).
<div n="NI"/>(viii) Hit by the arrow of Arjuna, Vikarṇa was wounded
<div n="lb"/>and he fell down from the chariot. (M.B. Virāṭa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 51, Stanza 41).
<div n="NI"/>(ix) On the first day of the battle of Bhārata, Vikarṇa
<div n="lb"/>entered in a combat with Śrutasoma. (M.B. Bhīṣma
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 45, Stanza 58).
<div n="NI"/>(x) He fought with Sahadeva. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 71, Stanza 21).
<div n="NI"/>(xi) Abhimanyu defeated Vikarṇa. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 78, Stanza 21).
<div n="NI"/>(xii) Ghaṭotkaca defeated Vikarṇa. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 92, Stanza 36).
[Page854-b+ 65]
<div n="NI"/>(xiii) There was a combat between Nakula and Vikarṇa.
<div n="lb"/>(Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 110, Stanza 11).
<div n="NI"/>(xiv) He fought with Bhīmasena. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 113).
<div n="NI"/>(xv) He fought with Śikhaṇḍī. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>96, Stanza 31).
<div n="NI"/>(xvi) He fought with Nakula and was defeated. (Droṇa
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 107, Stanza 30).
<div n="NI"/>(xvii) Bhīmasena killed him. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>137, Stanza 29).
