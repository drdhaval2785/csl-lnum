SUHVA¦. (SUHMA) A king of the Bharata dynasty.
<div n="lb"/>Suhva was the son of Bali, and grandson of Sutapas.
<div n="lb"/>Bali had no direct sons. Being miserable due to the
<div n="lb"/>absence of sons, Bali approached the hermit Dīrghata-
<div n="lb"/>mas, by whom the queen gave birth to six sons. They
<div n="lb"/>were Aṅga, Vanga, Kaliṅga, Suhva (or Suhma), Puṇḍra
<div n="lb"/>and Adrūpa. (Bhāgavata, Skandha 9).
