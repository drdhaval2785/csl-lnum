MAHĀVĪRA I¦. A son of Priyavrata. Svāyambhuva
<div n="lb"/>Manu had two famous sons named Priyavrata and
<div n="lb"/>Uttānapāda. Of them, Priyavrata married Surūpā and
<div n="lb"/>Barhiṣmatī, the two beautiful and virtuous daughters of
<div n="lb"/>Viśvakarma Prajāpati. By his first wife Surūpā, Priya-
<div n="lb"/>vrata had ten sons, namely, Agnīdhra, Idhmajihva,
<div n="lb"/>Yajñabāhu, Mahāvīra, Rukmaśukra, Ghṛtapṛṣṭha,
<div n="lb"/>Savana, Medhātithi, Vītihotra and Kavi. The youngest
<div n="lb"/>of his children was a daughter named Ūrjjasvatī. (Devī
<div n="lb"/>Bhāgavata, 8th Skandha).
