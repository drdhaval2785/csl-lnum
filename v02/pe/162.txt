ALAMBALA¦. A giant who used to eat human flesh.
<div n="lb"/>This cannibal was the son of Jaṭāsura. This asura
<div n="lb"/>(Alambala) fought on the side of the Kauravas in the
<div n="lb"/>Kurukṣetra battle because Bhīmasena had killed his
<div n="lb"/>father, Jaṭāsura. In the battle, Ghaṭotkaca cut off the
<div n="lb"/>head of this mighty warrior and magician and threw his
<div n="lb"/>head into the war-chariot of Duryodhana. (M.B., Droṇa
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 149).
