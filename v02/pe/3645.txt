KRODHAVAŚĀ¦. Wife of Prajāpati Kaśyapa. (Vālmīki
<div n="lb"/>Rāmāyaṇa, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, Sarga 14). The Asuras who
<div n="lb"/>were born to Krodhavaśā are also called Krodhavaśas.
<div n="lb"/>Most of these Krodhavaśas were employed to guard the
<div n="lb"/>lotus-lake of Kubera. (M.B. Vana Parva, Chapter 154).
<div n="lb"/>Bhīmasena once entered the lotus-lake of Kubera and
<div n="lb"/>plucked the “Saugandhika” flower. The Krodhavaśas
[Page418-b+ 64]
<div n="lb"/>ran to Kubera to inform him of this theft. It is stated
<div n="lb"/>in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 285, Stanza 2
<div n="lb"/>that these Krodhavaśas were present in the army of
<div n="lb"/>Rāvaṇa.
