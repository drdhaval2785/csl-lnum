TĀMRA¦. A demon, son of Murāsura. The demon
<div n="lb"/>Mura had seven children named Tāmra, Antarīkṣa,
<div n="lb"/>Śravaṇa, Vasu, Vibhāvasu, Nabhasvān and Aruṇa. Of
<div n="lb"/>these Tāmra was Mahiṣāsura's minister for a long time.
<div n="lb"/>The Chief Minister of Mahiṣāsura was Asilomā;
<div n="lb"/>Defence Minister, Cikṣura; Foreign minister, Viḍāla
<div n="lb"/>and Finance minister, Tāmra. Udarka was the
<div n="lb"/>Commander-in-chief and Bāṣkala, Trinetra and
<div n="lb"/>Kālabandhaka were Cabinet Ministers. He was killed
<div n="lb"/>along with his father Mura, by Śrī Kṛṣṇa. (10th
<div n="lb"/>Skandha Bhāgavata and 5th Skandha, Devī Bhāgavata).
