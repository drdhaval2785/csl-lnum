{%prayāṇa-daṇḍa%}¦ (IE 8-5; EI 25, 26), cf. {%rājasevakānāṃ vasati-%}
<div n="lb">{%daṇḍa-prayāṇa-daṇḍau na staḥ;%} refers to the obligation of the
<div n="lb">villagers to provide free conveyance and carriage of loads, etc.,
<div n="lb">when royal officers passed through their village. {%Prayāṇa%} thus
<div n="lb">seems to mean ‘passage, transit’, and {%daṇḍa%} ‘levy’; cf. {%rūḍhabhāroḍi.%}
