{%śulka%}¦ (IE 8-5; EI 12, 23, 25, 30; CII 4; CITD), tolls;
<div n="lb">customs duties; octroi duties; taxes particularly levied at ferries
<div n="lb">and passes and on roads, etc.; cf. {%krīḍara-śulka%} (EI 5), name of
<div n="lb">a tax; also {%śuṅgam, śuṅkam%} (SITI), customs, tolls.
<div n="P">({%HRS%}), tax, probably identical with {%bali%} according to
<div n="lb">early authorities; ferry dues, tolls and transit duties according
<div n="lb">to the {%Smṛtis%} and lexicons; according to the {%Arthaśāstra,%} (1)
<div n="lb">duties levied upon articles imported into a city, (2) port-dues,
<div n="lb">(3) duty upon the sale of liquors, (4) customs collected by
<div n="lb">the ferrymen and boundary-officers, (5) duty upon mining
<div n="lb">products, (6) duty upon imported salt, and (7) duty upon
<div n="lb">animals intended for slaughter; according to Śukra, tolls and
<div n="lb">duties upon building-sites, etc. The {%Amarakoṣa%} explains {%śulka%}
<div n="lb">as {%ghaṭṭ-ādi-deya%} and Kṣīrasvāmin enumerates the dues as ferry
<div n="lb">duties, tolls paid at military stations or police outposts and
<div n="lb">transit duties paid by merchants.
