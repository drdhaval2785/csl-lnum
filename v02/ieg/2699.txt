<div n="lb">cultivable land created from the bed of a river which carries
<div n="lb">the flood-water from the sea; a salty marsh or meadow; a
<div n="lb">rice field created near the bed of a nullah on the sea shore
<div n="lb">by putting embankments. See {%Ep. Ind.,%} Vol. XXXIII, pp.
<div n="lb">53-54. It is sometimes called {%pukkoli-khajjana, pukkoli%} probably
