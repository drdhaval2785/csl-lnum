{#antar#}¦ ind. (see {#nipAta, gati, upasarga#}). A particle implying
<div n="lb"><sup>1</sup> within, in the middle, <sup>2</sup> between, <sup>3</sup> under, in its literal and
<div n="lb">metaphorical acceptations. It is used either adverbially or as
<div n="lb">a separable preposition or as a prefix to verbs and occurs
<div n="lb">also, but seldom, in the sense of a noun. With nouns, if
<div n="lb">preceding them it may form Tatpur., Bahuvr. and Avyayībh.,
<div n="lb">if following them indecl. Tatpur. compounds. It means:
<div n="P">I. (if used adverbially) <sup>1</sup> In the middle, within; mostly
<div n="lb">followed or preceded by a noun in the locat.; e. g. {#apsvanta-#}
<div n="lb">{#ramftam#}. <sup>2</sup> In the interior (said of a kingdom &c.); e. g.
<div n="lb">{#laGuvfttitayA BidAM gataM vahirantaSca#}--comm. {#vahirmitrAdi-#}
<div n="lb">{#janapadezu . antaramAtyAdizu--nfpasya maRqalam . aBiBUya#}
<div n="lb">{#haratyanantaraH#} &c.). <sup>3</sup> In the mind, in the heart, e. g. {#aByu-#}
<div n="lb">{#dayAnantarasAvaDita mudA nEzaDapriyAnantarasA#}. <sup>4</sup> By way of
[Page118-b+ 56]
<div n="lb">enclosing or seizing, e. g. {#antarhatvA mUzikAM Syeno gataH#}
<div n="lb">(comm. = {#parigfhya#}); comp. III. 1. [This meaning is meant
<div n="lb">by the native Koshas when they render {#antar#} by {#svI-#}
<div n="lb">{#kAre,#} for the Kaśikā interprets Pāṇini I. 4. 65. {#‘parigrahaH#}
<div n="lb">{#svIkaraRam’;#} Mathureśa has probably misunderstood the
<div n="lb">bearing of {#svIkaraRa#} when substituting for the latter word
<div n="lb">the interpretation {#aNgIkftO#}.]
<div n="P">II. (as a separable preposition) <sup>1</sup> Within, <sup>2</sup> Between;
<div n="lb">followed or preceded <sup>a.</sup> by the genitive, e. g. {#tasmAnnAkzyA-#}
<div n="lb">{#dityayorantaH parameSvaraH;#} or {#hiraRmayyoH kuSyorantaravahita Asa;#}
<div n="lb"><sup>b.</sup> (ved.) by the accus., e. g. {#rodasI antarurvI#}.
<div n="P">III. (as a prefix to verbs) <sup>1</sup> In the middle, between, e. g.
<div n="lb">{#antarhatya#} ‘having struck in the middle’; compare on the
<div n="lb">contrary {#antarhatvA#} 1. 3. <sup>2</sup> Under (implying disappearance);
<div n="lb">e. g. {#DA#} with {#antar#} to hide, to make invisible, comp. also
<div n="lb">{#antarDi, antarDA#} &c. [The intimate relation between {#antar#}
<div n="lb">and the radical or verb in the latter cases is indicated not
<div n="lb">only by the influence this particle exercises on the meaning
<div n="lb">of the radical and its derivatives, but also by the effect it
<div n="lb">has on the formation of the gerund (comp. {#antarhatvA#} I. 4.
<div n="lb">and {#antarhatya#} III. 1.) and by the change it may produce in
<div n="lb">the dental {#n#} of the radical and its derivation affixes, comp.
<div n="lb">e. g. {#antarayaRa, antarhaRana#} &c.]
<div n="P">IV. (as an indeclinable noun) <sup>1</sup> The interior, any thing
<div n="lb">not belonging to the exterior world, e. g. {#antarvahiriti#}
<div n="lb">{#kAryadravyasya kAraRAntaravacanAdakArye tadaBAvaH;#} or {#Buvo'nta-#}
<div n="lb">{#rUrDvamaDaSca#}. <sup>2</sup> The heart, e. g. {#rAkzama Bakza mAM…antaradAm#}
<div n="lb">{#(= hfdayaM dattavatI)#}. Compare also {#antarvat#} and {#antarA#}.
<div n="P">V. (in composition with nouns) In the middle of, in the in-
<div n="lb">terior of, in the heart of; <sup>1</sup> before the noun: <sup>a.</sup> in Tatpur., e.
<div n="lb">g. {#antarja, antarduzwa;#} in several comp. of this class, it may
<div n="lb">seem as if the latter part depended on {#antar#} in the genitive,
<div n="lb">e. g. {#antaHSarIra#} the interior of the body, {#antarhfdaya#} the
<div n="lb">interior of the heart, but it is more correct to consider
<div n="lb">these interpretations as arising from such as ‘the body
<div n="lb">in its interior &c.’; comp. several comp. beginning with
<div n="lb">{#agra,#} e. g. {#agranaKa, agrahasta#} &c.; the {#n#} is changed to
<div n="lb">{#Ra#} in the Tatpur. {#antarvaRa#} and {#antarayaRa#} qq. vv.; see also
<div n="lb">{#antarIpa#}. <sup>b.</sup> in Bahuvr., e. g. {#antaHsveda, antarvAzpa;#} some-
<div n="lb">times meaning ‘from the interior’, e. g. {#antaHsuKa, antaHprajYa#}.
<div n="lb"><sup>c.</sup> in Avyayībh., e. g. {#antarveSmam, antargiri#}. <sup>2</sup> After the
<div n="lb">noun, in indecl. Tatpur., e. g. {#te nAlikerAntarapaH pibantaH;#} or
<div n="lb">{#jalAntaScandracapalam;#} or {#udarAntaH praviSya;#} in such compounds
<div n="lb">some comm. consider the former part as representing the
<div n="lb">sense of a locative, not of a genitive ({#‘avyayatvAtzazWIsamA-#}
<div n="lb">{#sapratizeDaH’#}), others not; e. g. {#saMpravezwumiva yozita IzuH Sli-#}
<div n="lb">{#zyatAM hfdayamizwatamAnAm . AtmanaH satatameva tadantarvartino na#}
<div n="lb">{#Kalu nUnamajAnan#} (comm. {#tezAmizwatamAnAmantarhfdaye#}).
