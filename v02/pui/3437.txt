<div n="lb"/>gate of Mathurā by Kṛṣṇa. Defeated Kūpakarṇa, a minis-
<div n="lb"/>ter of Bāṇa; was sent to Hāstinapura by Kṛṣṇa to find out

<div n="lb"/>take the jewel Syamantaka (s.v.) as he did not give his

<div n="lb"/>horse of Kṛṣṇa.<sup>2</sup> After the {%Rājasūya,%} he was sent to Dvā-
<div n="lb"/>rakā for its defence.<sup>3</sup> Went to Upalāvya to see the Pāṇḍa-
<div n="lb"/>vas, and to Syamantapañcaka for solar eclipse.<sup>4</sup> His son
<div n="lb"/>was married to Cārumatī, a daughter of Rukminī.<sup>5</sup> Survived
<div n="lb"/>Kurukṣetra war;<sup>6</sup> Heard of Śatadhanva's death at Kṛṣṇa's
