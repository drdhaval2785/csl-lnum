<div n="lb"/>Akṣaras, and out of his four mouths appeared the four Vedas,

<div n="lb"/>and Ātman. In these Hari manifests himself: its metres are
<div n="lb"/>Gāyatrī, Uṣṇik and so on.<sup>2</sup> Symbolised by Garuḍa: do not

<div n="lb"/>see the Trivikrama form of Hari.<sup>4</sup> Learnt by Balarāma and
