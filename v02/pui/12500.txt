{%Vātaskandha%}¦ — There are seven Vātaskandhas each
<div n="lb"/>with a Marut gaṇa of seven. In the first the seven are Śakra-
<div n="lb"/>jyoti, Satya, Satyajyoti, Citrajyoti, Jyotiṣmān, Sutapas and
<div n="lb"/>Caitya; Ṛtajit, Satyajit, Suṣeṇa, Senajit, Sutamitra, Amitra
<div n="lb"/>and Suramitra are in the second; in the third Dhātu, Dhanada,
<div n="lb"/>Ugra, Bhīma, Varuṇa, Abhiyuktākṣika and Sāhvaya; in
<div n="lb"/>the fourth, the names of the gaṇa are omitted in the text;
<div n="lb"/>in the fifth Īdṛk, Anyādṛk, Sasariddrumavṛkṣakas, Mita
<div n="lb"/>and Samita; in the sixth, Īdṛk Puruṣa, Nānyādṛk, Sama-
<div n="lb"/>cetana, Sammita, Samavṛtti, and Pratiharta; in the seventh,
<div n="lb"/>the names not clear.
