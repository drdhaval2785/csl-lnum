{%Dāruka%} (I)¦ — the charioteer of Kṛṣṇa;<sup>1</sup> went with
<div n="lb"/>Kṛṣṇa to Śālva's station, and drove the chariot when Kṛṣṇa
<div n="lb"/>gained Lakṣmaṇā by svayamvara;<sup>2</sup> took Kṛṣṇa to Mithilā
<div n="lb"/>and the Videha country.<sup>3</sup> His son was the charioteer of
<div n="lb"/>Pradyumna.<sup>4</sup> Stood before Kṛṣṇa on the eve of His return-
<div n="lb"/>ing from the earth. Kṛṣṇa asked him to inform the Dvārakā
<div n="lb"/>people of the position of the Yādavas at Prabhāsa and advise
<div n="lb"/>them to go to Indraprastha with Arjuna. Returning to the
<div n="lb"/>city, Dāruka informed Vasudeva and others accordingly.<sup>5</sup>
