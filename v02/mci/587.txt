<div n="lb">(a particular arrangement of the army)
<div n="lb">7. 6. 15.
<div n="P">Droṇa arranged the Kaurava army in the
<div n="lb">form of the Śakaṭavyūha on the eleventh day
<div n="lb">of the war; Jayadratha, the Kaliṅga king (not
<div n="lb">named), and Vikarṇa stood on the right side
<div n="lb">({%dakṣiṇa pārśva%}); at the extremity of the
<div n="lb">right wing ({%prapakṣa%}) was Śakuni, assisted
<div n="lb">by excellent horsemen from the Gandhāra
[Page144-b+ 40]
<div n="lb">country who fought with sharp {%prāsas;%} Kṛpa,
<div n="lb">Kṛtavarman, Citrasena, and Viviṃśati, with
<div n="lb">Duḥśāsana to lead them ({%duḥśāsanamukhāḥ%}),
<div n="lb">stood on its left side ({%savya pārśva%}); at the
<div n="lb">extremity of the left wing ({%prapakṣa%}) stood
<div n="lb">the Kāmbojas, led by Sudakṣiṇa, who fought
<div n="lb">on horses, as also the Śakas and the Yavanas;
<div n="lb">apparently in between the two sides were
<div n="lb">stationed the Madras, the Trigartas, the
<div n="lb">Ambaṣṭhas, warriors who came from the west
<div n="lb">and the north, the Śibis, the Śūrasenas, the
<div n="lb">Śūdras, the Maladas, the Sauvīras, and the
<div n="lb">Kitavas, all of whom were led by Duryo-
<div n="lb">dhana ({%tavātmajaṃ puraskṛtya%}); Karṇa
<div n="lb">took position in front of Duryodhana
<div n="lb">and was at the head of all the archers
<div n="lb">({%pramukhe sarvadhanvinām%}) 7. 6. 1-8;
<div n="lb">in this Vyūha, Karṇa was in front of the army
<div n="lb">({%tāvakānāṃ mukhaṃ karṇaḥ%}) 7. 6. 21;
<div n="lb">from the description of the start of that
<div n="lb">day's battle it is clear that Droṇa also stood
<div n="lb">at the head of the army 7. 6. 32-36; this
<div n="lb">Vyūha of the Kauravas was opposed by the
<div n="lb">Pāṇḍavas with the Krauñcavyūha 7. 6. 15.
