{#yuDizWira#}¦ m. ({#-raH#}) The elder of the five {%Pāṇḍava%} princes,
and leader in the great war, between them and the {%Kurus,%} in the beginning of
the fourth age; the nominal son of PĀṆḌU, whom he succeeded in the
sovereignty of {%India,%} but according to the legend, begotten on {%Kunti%} by
the deity YAMA.
.E. {#yuDi#} in war or battle, and {#zWira#} for {#sTira#} firm; it is also
sometimes read {#yudGasTira#}.
