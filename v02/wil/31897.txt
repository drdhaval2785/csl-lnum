{#raTayAtrA#}¦ f. ({#-trA#}) A festival at which the chariots of the gods go
in procession: in Bengal and Orissa it is applied to the procession of the car

{%Āṣārha.%}
.E. {#raTa#}, and {#yAtrA#} going.
