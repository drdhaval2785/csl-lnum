.²1 Fixed or permanent condition.
.²2 A feeling or passion, which is the object of poetical or dramatic
description, and is classed sometimes as one of three, but more usually as one
of two orders of such objects; this order being opposed especially to the
{%Vyabhicārī Bhāvas:%} the feelings or conditions termed {%Sthāyi%} are
eight, viz. desire, mirth, sorrow, heroism, anger, fear, aversion, and
astonishment.
.E. {#sTAyin#} fixed, and {#BAva#} property.
