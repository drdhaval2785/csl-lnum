{#karmman#}¦ mn. ({#-rmmA-rmma#})
.²1 Action in general.
.²2 The object of an action.
.²3 The subject of action in grammar, considered of three kinds, {#prApya#} or
{#vyApya#} when it is simple, as {#grAmaM gacCati#} he goes to the village;
{#sUryyaM paSyati#} he sees the sun; {#vikAryya#} when change of form is
implied, as {#suvarRaM kuRqalaM karoti#} gold forms the ear-ring; and
{#nirvartya#} when some new product is evolved, as {#kawaM karoti#} he makes a
mat; {#putraM prasUte#} she bears a son.
.²4 The objective noun.
.²5 Action specific, moral duty, the obligation imposed by peculiarities of
tribe, occupation, &c.
.²6 Natural, active property, as maturity, of heat; support, of earth;
dispersion, of wind, &c.
.²7 Religious action, as sacrifice, ablution, &c. especially as originating in
the hope of future recompense, and as opposed to speculative religion or
knowledge of spirit.
.²8 (In Logic,) Substantial action or motion of five kinds, upwards, downwards,
extension, contraction, and going in general.
.²9 Action or act involving retribution or reward, virtue, vice, &c.
.²10 Fate, the certain consequence of previous acts.
