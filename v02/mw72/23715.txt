<div n="lb"/>valent to ‘not,’ ‘no,’ ‘nor,’ ‘neither’ [cf. {%no, na-%}
<div n="lb"/>{%nu, na-hi, na-tarām%}], and in the Veda a particle of

<div n="lb"/>{%na tvāṃ śapet,%} lest he should curse thee). In
