<div n="lb"/>of a teacher, a pupil of {@Pārāśarya,@} in the first Vaṃśa (list of
<div n="lb"/>teachers) in the Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad.<sup>1</sup> A Prācīnayogya
<div n="lb"/>is mentioned also in the Chāndogya<sup>2</sup> and the Taittirīya<sup>3</sup>

<div n="lb"/>Brāhmaṇa<sup>4</sup> and in the Jaiminīya Upaniṣad Brāhmaṇa (see
<div n="lb"/>{@Puluṣa, Satyayajña,@}<sup>5</sup> {@Somaśuṣma@}).

<F>2) v. 13, 1.</F>
