<div n="lb"/>‘general.’ He is mentioned in the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> where also<sup>2</sup> the
<div n="lb"/>word is used metaphorically. He is also referred to in the
<div n="lb"/>Śatarudriya,<sup>3</sup> as well as elsewhere in the Yajurveda Saṃhitās and
<div n="lb"/>Brāhmaṇas.<sup>4</sup> He is one of the {@Ratnins@} of the king.<sup>5</sup> Presum-
<div n="lb"/>ably he was appointed by the king, not by the people, to
<div n="lb"/>command in war when the king became too important to lead
<div n="lb"/>every little fray in person. In the Aitareya Brāhmaṇa<sup>6</sup> this
<div n="lb"/>official is called Senā-pati.
