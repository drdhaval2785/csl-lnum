<div n="lb"/>is referred to. The Gandhāris are also mentioned with the

<div n="lb"/>times on the south bank of the {@Kubhā@} up to its mouth in the
<div n="lb"/>Indus, and for some distance down the east side of the Indus
<div n="lb"/>itself. They later formed a portion of the Persian empire,
