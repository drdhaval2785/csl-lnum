{@Bilva@}¦ is the name of the wood-apple tree ({%Aigle marmelos%}).
<div n="lb"/>It is mentioned in the Brāhmaṇas<sup>1</sup> and in the Atharvaveda,<sup>2</sup>
<div n="lb"/>where a reference to its valuable fruit may be intended.
<div n="lb"/>According to the Taittirīya Saṃhitā,<sup>3</sup> the sacrificial post was
<div n="lb"/>made of Bilva wood in some cases. The Śāṅkhāyana Āraṇ-
<div n="lb"/>yaka<sup>4</sup> contains a hymn in praise of the virtues of an amulet of
<div n="lb"/>Bilva ({%irā-maṇi bailva%}).<sup>5</sup>
