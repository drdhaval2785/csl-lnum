<div n="lb"/>Homeric <lang n="greek">γέρας</lang> This sense also suits Nirāja, the variant of
<div n="lb"/>both the Kāṭhaka<sup>5</sup> and the Kapiṣṭhala<sup>6</sup> Saṃhitās.
