{@Balbaja@}¦ is the name of the grass called {%Eleusine indica.%} It
<div n="lb"/>is mentioned in the Atharvaveda,<sup>1</sup> and is said in the Yajurveda
<div n="lb"/>Saṃhitās<sup>2</sup> to be produced from the excrements of cattle. In
<div n="lb"/>the Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā<sup>2</sup> it is stated to be used for the sacrificial
<div n="lb"/>litter ({@Barhis@}) and for fuel. Baskets or other products made
<div n="lb"/>from this grass are referred to in a Dānastuti (‘Praise of Gifts’)
<div n="lb"/>in the Rigveda.<sup>3</sup>
