{@Śauṣkala@}¦ is the name of one of the sacrificial victims at the
<div n="lb"/>Puruṣamedha (‘horse sacrifice’) in the Yajurveda.<sup>1</sup> It means,
<div n="lb"/>according to the St. Petersburg Dictionary, ‘living on dried fish

<div n="lb"/>hook, ‘angler.’

<div n="lb"/>tirīya Brāhmaṇa, iii. 4, 12, 1. {%Cf.%} Weber,
<div n="lb"/>{%Indische Streifen,%} 1, 81, n. 7; Eggeling,
