{@Try-āśir,@}¦ ‘with three admixtures,’ is an epithet of Soma in
<div n="lb"/>the Rigveda.<sup>1</sup> According to Sāyaṇa this means mixed with
<div n="lb"/>curds ({@Dadhi@}), meal ({@Saktu@}), and milk ({@Payas@}). More accurately

<div n="lb"/>and the curds ({%dadhyāśir%}), which were used to mix with the
<div n="lb"/>Soma.
