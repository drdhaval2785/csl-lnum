<div n="lb"/>personage who is mentioned in the Rigveda and later as a Ṛṣi,
<div n="lb"/>and more specially as the inventor of agriculture<sup>4</sup> and the lord

<div n="lb"/>passages<sup>6</sup> the epithet Vainya, ‘descendant of Vena,’ and must
<div n="lb"/>probably be regarded as a culture hero rather than as a real
<div n="lb"/>man. According to other accounts,<sup>7</sup> he was the first of con-
