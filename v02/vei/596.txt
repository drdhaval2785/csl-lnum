{@Kāla,@}¦ the generic expression for ‘time,’ first occurs in the
<div n="lb"/>Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> where, however, it is used only once, in the late
<div n="lb"/>tenth book. It is known to the Atharvaveda,<sup>2</sup> where Kāla has
<div n="lb"/>already developed the sense of time as fate. The word is
<div n="lb"/>frequent in the Brāhmaṇas,<sup>3</sup> superseding the earlier use of {@Ṛtu.@}

<div n="lb"/>see {@Ahan, Māsa, Saṃvatsara.@}
