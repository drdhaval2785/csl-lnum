{@Sveda-ja,@}¦ ‘born of sweat’ — that is, ‘engendered by hot
<div n="lb"/>moisture’ — is used in the Aitareya Upaniṣad (iii. 3, 3) as a term
<div n="lb"/>designating a class of creatures comprising vermin of all sorts.
<div n="lb"/>The Mānava Dharma Śāstra (i. 45) explains it as ‘flies,
<div n="lb"/>mosquitoes, lice, bugs, and so forth.’
