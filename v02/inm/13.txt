{@Abhimanyu,@}¦ son of Arjuna and Subhadrā, sister of Kṛṣṇa
<div n="lb">(I, {@2,@} 401; {@63,@} 2449; {@95,@} 3830; {@221,@} 8026), is an incarna-
<div n="lb">tion of Varcas, son of Soma (I, {@67,@} 2748), wherefore he enters
<div n="lb">Soma after death, after his Karman has elapsed (XVIII, {@5,@}
<div n="lb">165); (false) etymology of his name (I, {@221,@} 8027); he is
<div n="lb">the vaṃśakara of the Pāṇḍavas (I, {@95,@} 3834). In the
[Page001-b+ 47]
<div n="lb">beginning of the exile of the Pāṇḍavas he is carried to
<div n="lb">Dvārakā with his mother by Kṛṣṇa (III, {@22,@} 895), where
<div n="lb">he is educated and protected by Raukmiṇeya (III, {@183,@}
<div n="lb">12584 and 12586; cf. {@235,@} 14733). After the elapse of
<div n="lb">the exile he joins the Pāṇḍavas in Upaplavya with Kṛṣṇa
<div n="lb">and Subhadrā (IV, {@72;@} 2358, cf. 2350), and is there married
<div n="lb">to Uttarā, daughter of king Virāṭa (I, {@2,@} 489; IV., {%72,%}
<div n="lb">2355; V, 1). He fights bravely in the great battle, until he
<div n="lb">on the 13th day, when enjoined by Yudhiṣṭhira to break
<div n="lb">Droṇa's cakravyūha (VII, {@35,@} 1519), is surrounded and
<div n="lb">killed by Jayadratha and his people, leaving his wife Uttarā
<div n="lb">with a yet unborn son, Parikṣit, the only one left to
<div n="lb">propagate the race of the Pāṇḍavas. At first he is not
<div n="lb">seen in Heaven by Yudhiṣṭhira (XVIII, {@1,@} 26), but later
<div n="lb">he is shown to him beside Soma in Heaven (XVIII, {@4,@} 146).
